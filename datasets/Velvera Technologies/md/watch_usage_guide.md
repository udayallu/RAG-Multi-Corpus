# Velvera Watch Series — Usage Guide

## Getting Started

**1. What’s in the Box**
- Velvera Watch (choose your model)
- Magnetic Charging Cable (Standard/Deluxe) or Wireless Charger (Deluxe/Premium)
- Quick Start Guide
- Additional Ocean Band Strap (included with Premium model)

**2. Charging the Watch**
- Connect the magnetic charger or place the watch on the wireless charging pad.
- Attach the charger to a certified power adapter (5V/2A recommended).
- The watch will vibrate briefly and display the charging symbol.
- Fast charging available: achieve 50% charge in 30 minutes (Standard 42mm); 80% in 40 minutes (Premium 49mm).

## Setting Up Your Velvera Watch

**1. Power On**
- Press and hold the side button until the Velvera logo appears.

**2. Pairing With Your Smartphone**
- On your phone, download the “Velvera App” from Google Play or Apple App Store.
- Enable Bluetooth and open the app.
- Tap “Add Device” and select your Velvera Watch model.
- Confirm the pairing code shown on both devices.

**3. Device Compatibility**
- Requires Android 9.0+ or iOS 13+.
- Ensure Bluetooth and location services are enabled for seamless connectivity.

## Wearing Your Watch

- Strap your Velvera Watch snugly on your wrist, about 1 finger’s width above your wrist bone.
- Adjust straps for comfort—choose between Fluoroelastomer, Leather, Milanese Loop, or Ocean Band (additional straps sold separately).
- For water activities or diving (Premium 49mm), secure the Ocean Band strap provided.

## Daily Operations

### Navigating the Watch

- **Side Button**: Wake screen, open apps, long-press for voice assistant.
- **Swipe Gestures**:
  - Up/Down: View notifications, control center.
  - Left/Right: Access widgets and customizable watch faces.
- **Always-On Display**: Enabled by default. Adjust in Settings > Display.

### Notifications

- Receive calls, messages, emails, and app alerts instantly.
- Swipe down to open Notification Center.
- Tap to reply (preset replies on Android, voice input on iOS/Android).
- Customize notification preferences within the Velvera App.

## Health and Fitness Features

### Monitoring Health

- **Heart Rate**: Continuous monitoring with reminders on abnormal readings.
- **Sleep Tracking**: Auto-detects sleep stages (Deep, Light, REM); view summaries in the Velvera Health App.
- **Step Counter**: Tracks steps, calories, and distance daily.
- **ECG (Deluxe & Premium)**: Open ECG app, rest your finger on the side sensor for 30 seconds to record.
- **Blood Oxygen (SpO₂)**: Tap SpO₂ icon, keep wrist steady; results appear in 15 seconds.
- **Stress Detection (Deluxe & Premium)**: Review stress index and receive guided breathing exercises.
- **Skin Temperature (Premium 49mm)**: Measured periodically while worn; alerts for abnormal variations.

### Fitness Tracking

- Select activity from Multi-Sport Modes (run, swim, bike, yoga, etc.).
- For outdoor activities, enable integrated GPS (Deluxe & Premium).
- Swimming & Diving (Premium 49mm only): Water resistance up to 100m, records dive depth, duration, and water temperature.

## Connectivity Features

- **Bluetooth**: Standard (v5.2/5.3, model-specific); auto reconnects to paired device.
- **LTE (Deluxe/Premium)**: Activate embedded SIM via carrier for untethered calls/messages.
- **Satellite (Premium only)**: For location sharing and SOS functionality in remote areas.
- **Wi-Fi & NFC**: Enable in Settings for app downloads, wireless payments, and quick device transfers.

## Care & Maintenance

- Clean regularly using a soft, lint-free cloth.
- For water exposure (swimming/diving), rinse gently with fresh water and dry before use.
- Avoid harsh chemicals to preserve finish (especially Gold, Rose Gold, Ocean Blue).
- For strap replacement, squeeze the release pins on the back and swap with compatible Velvera bands.

---

## Safety & Durability

- Rated 5ATM (50m, Standard/Deluxe) or 10ATM (100m, Premium) for water resistance; avoid pressing buttons underwater.
- Sapphire Crystal (Deluxe & Premium) ensures high scratch protection.
- Emergency SOS (Premium): Long-press side button for 5 seconds—initiates location sharing and notifies emergency contacts.

## Troubleshooting & Support

- For setup or pairing issues: Restart both watch and phone; ensure Bluetooth and permissions are active.
- If battery drains quickly: Check for always-on GPS, disable unused notifications or background apps.
- For warranty claims or premium concierge support: Visit www.velvera.com/support or call 1-800-VELVERA.
