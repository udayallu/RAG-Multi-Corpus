##  Environmental Commitment

At Velvera, sustainability is not an afterthought — it's part of our design philosophy. From ethical sourcing to green logistics, every step is optimized to minimize environmental impact without compromising performance or innovation.

---

###  Sustainability Initiatives

- **Carbon Neutral Packaging**  
  All Velvera packaging is made from **100% recycled paper**, printed with soy-based inks, and manufactured in **carbon-neutral facilities**.

- ** Plastic-Free Experience**  
  Zero plastic across packaging — including tapes, wraps, and trays. Instead, we use biodegradable fiber-based cushioning and compostable seals.

- ** Trade-In & Recycling Program**  
  Recycle your old Velvera or any brand’s device responsibly through our **TakeBack+** program.  
  - Instant trade-in value at [velvera.com/trade-in](https://velvera.com/trade-in)  
  - Certified e-waste recycling partners  
  - Earn up to ₹1,500 in green credits  

- **Energy Efficiency**  
  All Velvera chargers and power adapters exceed **90% energy efficiency** standards and are designed to minimize phantom power drain.

- **Conflict-Free Materials**  
  All key components (tantalum, tin, tungsten, and gold) are sourced from **conflict-free and ethically audited** supply chains.

- **Eco-Friendly Accessories**  
  Accessories like phone cases and watch bands made from **bio-polymer**, **plant-based leather**, and **recycled ocean plastic**.
