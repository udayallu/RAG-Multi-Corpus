# **Velvera Productivity Tools — Usage Guide**
---

### **Velvera Pencil**

* **Getting Started**: Attach to your Velvera Pad’s magnetic side dock to pair instantly. No additional app setup is required.
* **Tips for Effective Use**: Use light hand pressure for precise control. Double-tap near the tip to switch between pen and eraser in supported apps.
* **Charging & Maintenance**: Charge fully on the Pad’s dock (recharges in 1 hour). Replace tip as needed—keep spare tip handy in the box.
* **Safety & Care**: Store with magnetic cap, away from liquids. Avoid dropping to protect sensitivity.
* **Troubleshooting**: If not connecting, reattach to dock to reset pairing. Ensure latest Pad software is installed.

---

### **Velvera Keyboard**

* **Getting Started**: Align magnetic connector with Pad edge; attaches securely when contact is made.
* **Tips for Effective Use**: Adjust keyboard backlighting for ambient lighting. Enable touchpad gestures in settings for multi-touch support.
* **Charging & Maintenance**: Clean with a dry, lint-free cloth. Avoid exposure to excessive moisture or dust.
* **Safety & Care**: Detach before storing tablet in tight sleeves. Do not press keys aggressively.
* **Troubleshooting**: Keyboard unrecognized? Detach and reattach. Clean pogo pins with a dry cloth if needed.

---

### **Velvera Stand Pro**

* **Getting Started**: Unfold to desired angle (15°–85°). Place phone/tablet on the silicone grip pads.
* **Tips for Effective Use**: Use low angles for drawing/writing; steeper for video calls. Keep cable organized in the integrated channel for desk clarity.
* **Charging & Maintenance**: Plug in USB-C cable for Qi charger. Wipe with a soft cloth. Silicone pads can be cleaned with mild soap if needed.
* **Safety & Care**: Do not exceed recommended device size. Ensure the stand is on a flat, stable surface.
* **Troubleshooting**: Wireless charging slow? Check charger cable connection or reposition device for better coil alignment.
