# Velvera Pad Series — Usage Guide

## Getting Started

### 1. Unboxing and Setup
- **Included in the Box:** Velvera Pad, 33W/45W USB-C power adapter, USB-C cable, Quick Start Guide, SIM ejector tool (Pro models only)
- **Optional Accessories:** Velvera Pencil, Velvera Keyboard

#### Step-by-Step Setup:
1. **Power On:**  
   Press and hold the power button (right edge).
2. **Language & Region:**  
   Follow on-screen prompts to select your language and region.
3. **Wi-Fi/Network Connection:**  
   - All models: Connect to Wi-Fi 6/6E.
   - Pad Pro: Optionally insert nano SIM for 5G cellular network.
4. **Google Account Sign-In:**  
   Sign in to your Google account for Play Store and sync services.
5. **System Updates:**  
   Allow Velvera UI to check for and install available updates.

---

### 2. Charging Your Tablet
- **Charging Port:** USB-C (bottom left edge)
- **Supported Charging:**
  - Pad 11": Up to 33W fast charging, full charge ~2.5 hours
  - Pad Pro 12.9": Up to 45W ultra-fast charging, full charge ~2 hours
- **Battery Tips:** Use the included adapter for optimal charging speed. Avoid letting the battery fully deplete for longer life.

---

## Everyday Usage

### 3. Tablet Navigation

| Action                | Gesture/Button                           | Description                                             |
|-----------------------|------------------------------------------|---------------------------------------------------------|
| Home Screen           | Swipe up from bottom or tap Home         | Returns to main screen                                  |
| App Switcher          | Swipe up & hold, tap Recent Apps icon    | View all open apps, switch or close apps                |
| Back                  | Swipe from either side or tap Back       | Returns to previous screen                              |
| Quick Settings        | Swipe down from top right                | Access Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, brightness, etc.               |
| Notifications         | Swipe down from top left                 | View messages, alerts, app notifications                |

---

### 4. Productivity & Multitasking

#### Multi-Window & Multitasking
- **Split Screen:**  
  Open Recents, tap app icon, choose "Split screen." Select second app.
- **Floating Apps:**  
  Supported apps: Tap and hold from Recent Apps, drag to open as floating window (Pro models).
- **Wireless Dex Mode:**  
  (Pad Pro only) Enable from Quick Settings for a desktop-like interface on external displays.

#### Keyboard & Mouse Support
- Attach Velvera Keyboard magnetically; tablet auto-detects.
- Use Bluetooth mouse for enhanced productivity.

---

### 5. Stylus Functions (Velvera Pencil)

| Function              | How-To                                                              | Model Support          |
|-----------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------|-----------------------|
| Pair & Charge         | Snap Pencil to tablet edge (magnetic/wireless charging, Pro only)   | All models            |
| Notes & Drawing       | Open Notes or any drawing app, begin writing or sketching           | All models            |
| Quick Annotate        | Double tap Pencil (customizable in settings)                        | All models            |
| Pressure Sensitivity  | 4096 levels (gradual shading, precision lines)                      | All models            |
| Tilt Support          | Supports artistic shading and edge effects                          | All models            |  

---

### 6. Camera & Media

- **Open Camera App:** Tap Camera icon from Home/Apps.
- **Main Camera Functions:**
    - **Photo/Video:** Standard, HDR, Portrait options (switch via bottom menu)
    - **Ultra-Wide:** (Pad Pro) Use toggle to switch rear lenses.
    - **Center Stage:** (Pad Pro) Keeps you centered during video calls.
- **Gallery & Editing:** Photos save automatically; edit and share from Photos app.

---

### 7. Connectivity

| Feature            | How to Use / Access                                          | Models                |
|--------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------|-----------------------|
| Wi-Fi 6/6E         | Quick Settings > Wi-Fi icon; select network                  | All                   |
| 5G Cellular        | Insert SIM > Settings > Network & Internet                   | Pad Pro only          |
| USB-C & Display    | Connect USB peripherals, external displays (DisplayPort out) | Pad Pro models        |
| Bluetooth          | Pair in Settings > Bluetooth                                 | All                   |
| Miracast           | Quick Settings > Screen Cast                                 | Pad Pro only          |

---

### 8. Security & Privacy

- **Face Unlock:**  
  Register via Settings > Security > Face Unlock (IR sensor on Pro).
- **Fingerprint Unlock:**  
  (Pad Pro) Follow onscreen prompts under Settings > Security.
- **App Permissions:**  
  Control per-app access in Settings > Apps & Permissions.

---

### 9. System & Software

- **System Updates:**  
  Settings > System > Updates
- **Backup & Restore:**  
  Settings > System > Backup
- **Parental Controls:**  
  Settings > Digital Wellbeing > Parental Controls

---

### 10. Troubleshooting & Support

| Issue                                | Suggested Action                                          |
|--------------------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------|
| Tablet won’t power on                | Hold power for 8 seconds, then release; charge for 10 mins|
| Cannot connect to Wi-Fi              | Toggle Wi-Fi, check password, restart router               |
| Accessory not detected               | Reattach accessory, check for updates; restart device      |
| Rapid battery drain                  | Reduce screen brightness, close background apps            |
| Software lag or freeze               | Force restart; check for available updates                 |
| Need further help                    | Visit velvera.com/support or contact authorized service    |

## Accessory Usage

- **Velvera Pencil:** Attach magnetically to edge for charging and pairing; use for drawing or note-taking.

## Care & Maintenance

- Use soft, lint-free cloth to clean display.
- Avoid exposure to liquids, extreme heat, or direct sunlight.
- Store and charge tablet at room temperature when not in use.
