## Detailed Specifications

### 1. Velvera Pad 11"

**Models:** 128GB / 256GB  
**Display:**
- 11-inch IPS LCD, 2560 x 1600 resolution
- 120Hz refresh rate
- 500 nits peak brightness

**Processor:**
- Qualcomm Snapdragon 870

**Memory & Storage:**
- 8GB LPDDR4X RAM
- 128GB or 256GB UFS 3.1 Storage

**Operating System:**
- Velvera UI based on Android 13

**Connectivity:**
- Wi-Fi 6 (802.11ax)
- Bluetooth 5.2
- USB-C (USB 3.1 Gen1)

**Audio:**
- Dual stereo speakers, tuned for immersive sound
- 3.5mm headphone jack

**Cameras:**
- Rear: 13MP autofocus
- Front: 8MP wide-angle

**Battery:**
- 8,500mAh
- Up to 12 hours usage
- 33W fast charging

**Additional Features:**
- Face unlock
- Multi-window support
- Stylus (Velvera Pencil) support
- Keyboard accessory support

**Dimensions & Weight:**
- 260 x 164 x 6.9 mm
- 510g

---

### 2. Velvera Pad Pro 12.9"

**Models:** 256GB / 512GB / 1TB

**Display:**
- 12.9-inch OLED, 2800 x 1752 resolution
- 120Hz refresh rate
- HDR10+, 1000 nits peak brightness

**Processor:**
- Qualcomm Snapdragon 8+ Gen 1

**Memory & Storage:**
- 12GB LPDDR5 RAM (256GB or 512GB models)
- 16GB LPDDR5 RAM (1TB model)
- 256GB, 512GB, or 1TB UFS 3.1 Storage

**Operating System:**
- Velvera UI Pro based on Android 13

**Connectivity:**
- Wi-Fi 6E (802.11ax)
- 5G cellular connectivity (nano SIM & eSIM)
- Bluetooth 5.3
- USB-C (USB 3.2 Gen2), DisplayPort out

**Audio:**
- Quad stereo speakers, Dolby Atmos
- Dual microphones
- No headphone jack

**Cameras:**
- Rear: 16MP wide + 8MP ultra-wide
- Front: 12MP wide (Center Stage support)

**Battery:**
- 11,000mAh
- Up to 15 hours mixed usage
- 45W ultra-fast charging

**Security:**
- Face unlock (IR sensor)
- Under-display fingerprint sensor

**Additional Features:**
- Multi-tasking (split-screen, floating apps)
- Support for Velvera Pencil (4096 pressure levels, wireless charging)
- Magnetic keyboard & Smart Connector
- Wireless Dex Mode (desktop interface)
- Built-in screen recorder
- Wi-Fi Direct and Miracast support

**Dimensions & Weight:**
- 285 x 185 x 5.7 mm
- 650g
