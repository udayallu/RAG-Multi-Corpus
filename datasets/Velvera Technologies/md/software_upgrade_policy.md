Software support and feature availability vary by series to best match user needs — whether you're a power user, gamer, student, or first-time smartphone owner.

###  Update Timeline

Velvera ensures long-term device viability through a clear and transparent software lifecycle policy.

| Series          | Major OS Updates | Security Patches | End of Official Support |
|-----------------|------------------|------------------|--------------------------|
| **One Series**   | 4 Years          | 5 Years          | December 2030           |
| **Edge Series**  | 3 Years          | 4 Years          | October 2029            |
| **Nexus Series** | 4 Years          | 5 Years          | December 2030           |
| **Core Series**  | 2 Years          | 3 Years          | August 2028             |
| **Start Series** | 1 Year           | 2 Years          | March 2027              |

>  **Update Frequency**:  
> - **OS Upgrades**: Annually in Q2 (e.g., VelveraOS 7 in Q2 2026)  
> - **Security Patches**: Monthly (1st Monday of each month)  
> - **Bug Fixes & App Updates**: Rolled out bi-weekly via Velvera System Updater

---

###  Software Integrity & Privacy

All updates are:
- **Digitally Signed** and delivered over encrypted channels.
- **Privacy-Audited** and **Ad-Free**, in line with Velvera’s no-tracking policy.
- Rolled out in **phased batches**, with staged rollout status viewable in the "Updates" section of the device settings.

---

###  Upgrade Recommendations

- **For Power Users**: Stick with One or Nexus series to enjoy full feature continuity through OS 7, 8, and beyond.
- **For Budget Users**: Core and Start Series still get essential security updates and performance enhancements for peace of mind.
- **For Developers & Early Adopters**: Nexus series gets **Beta Access** to future VelveraOS versions through the **Velvera Labs Program**.

For update status by region or model, visit: [velvera.com/os-updates](https://velvera.com/os-updates)
