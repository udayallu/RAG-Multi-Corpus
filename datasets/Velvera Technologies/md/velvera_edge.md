
# Velvera Edge Series — Product Specification

Premium performance, perfect pocketability. The **Edge** series is ideal for users who prefer smaller phones without compromising on power.

---

## Overview

- **Series**: Compact Series — Velvera Edge
- **Target Audience**: Users preferring compact smartphones with flagship performance
- **Available Colors**: Cloud Gray, Midnight Ocean, Sage Green, Coral Pink, Arctic White
- **Display Type**: 6.1" OLED, Full HD+ Resolution, HDR10+

## Models & Variants

---

## Velvera Edge (128GB / 256GB)

### Common Features

- **Display**: 6.1-inch OLED, 2400x1080 pixels, 120Hz refresh rate, HDR10+
- **Processor**: Octa-core Velvera V5 Chip (4x2.8GHz + 4x1.9GHz)
- **GPU**: Mali V530 Advanced
- **Operating System**: VelveraOS 14 (Android 14 based)
- **Rear Cameras**:
  - 50MP f/1.8 Wide
  - 12MP f/2.2 Ultra-wide (120° FOV)
- **Front Camera**: 16MP f/2.0
- **Battery**: 4400mAh Li-Ion (non-removable)
- **Charging**: 33W Fast Charging (wired); 15W wireless charging
- **Biometrics**: Under-display fingerprint, Face Unlock
- **SIM**: Dual Nano SIM, eSIM support
- **Audio**: Stereo speakers, Dolby Atmos, No 3.5mm jack
- **Build**: Aluminum frame, Gorilla Glass 7 front & rear, IP68 water/dust resistant
- **Connectivity**: 5G SA/NSA, Wi-Fi 6, Bluetooth 5.3, NFC, USB-C 3.1
- **Dimensions**: 145.6 x 68.9 x 7.89 mm
- **Weight**: 162g
- **Other**: Haptic feedback engine, Linear vibration motor

- **Main Camera**: Additional 8MP f/2.4 Telephoto with OIS (3x Optical Zoom)
- **Battery**: 4600mAh Li-Ion (non-removable)
- **Charging**: 45W Fast Charging (wired); 18W wireless charging
- **OS**: VelveraOS 14 Pro (exclusive AI features)
