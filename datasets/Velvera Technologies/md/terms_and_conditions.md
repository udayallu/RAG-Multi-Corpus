###  Privacy & Data Security

Velvera is committed to safeguarding your digital rights, privacy, and data through a **privacy-first design philosophy** embedded in all our products and services.

####  Security Highlights

- **Hardware-Level Encryption**:  
  All Velvera devices include **secure enclaves and tamper-proof encryption modules**.

- **Local Data Processing**:  
  AI features like voice processing, photo indexing, and suggestions are executed **on-device by default**, reducing unnecessary data transmission to cloud servers.

- **Privacy Controls**:  
  - VelveraOS provides a **granular privacy dashboard** to control app permissions, background activity, and data access logs.
  - Mic/Camera indicators and hardware kill-switch options (Nexus series) enhance user control.

- **Data Compliance**:
  - Fully **GDPR**, **India PDP Act**, and **California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)** compliant.
  - Annual third-party audits and **white-hat bug bounty programs** ensure continuous hardening.

- **No Ad Tracking Policy**:
  - Velvera **does not monetize user data** or permit third-party behavioral ads across its ecosystem.
  - Anonymous telemetry (if enabled) is used **solely for performance improvement**.
