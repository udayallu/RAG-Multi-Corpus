**Service Process Overview:**
- **Service booking:** Initiate via Velvera App, website, or by contacting the nearest service center.
- **Diagnosis:** Certified engineers assess reported issues, typically within 24 hours of product receipt.
- **Quote & Approval:** Estimate shared for any non-warranty services or accidental damage; repairs begin post-approval.
- **Status Tracking:** Transparent updates through app, SMS, and email notifications.
- **Delivery & Feedback:** Products can be collected in person or delivered to the customer’s address upon request.

**Additional Support:**
- Same-day screen replacement at select centers for Velvera Care+ users (subject to slot availability).
- Genuine Velvera parts and 90-day post-repair warranty for paid repairs.
