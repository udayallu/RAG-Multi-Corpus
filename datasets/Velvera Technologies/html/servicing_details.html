Service Process Overview: - Service booking: Initiate via Velvera App, website, or by contacting the nearest service center. - Diagnosis: Certified engineers assess reported issues, typically within 24 hours of product receipt. - Quote & Approval: Estimate shared for any non-warranty services or accidental damage; repairs begin post-approval. - Status Tracking: Transparent updates through app, SMS, and email notifications. - Delivery & Feedback: Products can be collected in person or delivered to the customer’s address upon request.
