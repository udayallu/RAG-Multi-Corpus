Update Timeline

Velvera ensures long-term device viability through a clear and transparent software lifecycle policy.

Update Frequency:
- OS Upgrades: Annually in Q2 (e.g., VelveraOS 7 in Q2 2026)
- Security Patches: Monthly (1st Monday of each month)
- Bug Fixes & App Updates: Rolled out bi-weekly via Velvera System Updater

Software Integrity & Privacy

All updates are: - Digitally Signed and delivered over encrypted channels. - Privacy-Audited and Ad-Free, in line with Velvera’s no-tracking policy. - Rolled out in phased batches, with staged rollout status viewable in the “Updates” section of the device settings.

Upgrade Recommendations

  • For Power Users: Stick with One or Nexus series to enjoy full feature continuity through OS 7, 8, and beyond.
  • For Budget Users: Core and Start Series still get essential security updates and performance enhancements for peace of mind.
  • For Developers & Early Adopters: Nexus series gets Beta Access to future VelveraOS versions through the Velvera Labs Program.
