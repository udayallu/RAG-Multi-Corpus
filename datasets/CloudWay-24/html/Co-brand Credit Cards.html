Introducing CloudWay 24 Co-brand Credit Cards

Unlock the best way to travel and reward yourself with SkyPoints! Our exclusive co-brand credit cards, in partnership with leading banks, allow you to accelerate your SkyPoints earnings every time you spend.

SkyPoints Earning Structure

  • 10X SkyPoints
    Earn 10 times the SkyPoints when you book CloudWay 24 flights using your co-brand credit card on CloudWay24.com or our official app.

  • 4% SkyPoints
    Earn 4% back in SkyPoints on spending for other airlines and hotel bookings, worldwide.

  • 2% SkyPoints
    Earn 2% back in SkyPoints on all other spends—retail, dining, shopping, utilities, and more.

How to Apply?

  1. Visit CloudWay24.com or your preferred bank website.
  2. Choose the “CloudWay 24 Co-brand Credit Card.”
  3. Fill in the application form and provide necessary documents.
  4. Start earning SkyPoints as soon as your card is activated!
