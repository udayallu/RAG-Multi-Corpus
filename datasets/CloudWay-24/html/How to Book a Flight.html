How to Book a Flight with CloudWay 24

CloudWay 24 offers an easy and seamless booking experience via their website and mobile app (available for iOS and Android). Follow these steps to book your next international journey!

Step-by-Step Guide to Booking a Flight

1. Sign Up / Log In

  • Visit CloudWay24.com or open the CloudWay 24 app.
  • Sign up or log in using your email address or mobile number.

2. Start Your Booking

  • Click or tap on “Plan My Flight”.

3. Select Your Trip Type

  • Choose either:
    • Round Trip
    • One Way

4. Choose Your Route

  • Select your:
    • Source city (e.g., Mumbai, Delhi, Bengaluru)
    • Destination city (e.g., New York, London, Dubai, Sydney, etc.)
  • Enter preferred departure and (if round-trip) return dates.

5. Pick Your Cabin Class

  • CloudLux (First Class)
  • CloudElite (Business Class)
  • CloudComfort (Premium Economy)
  • CloudSaver (Economy)

6. Confirm Flight Options

  • Browse available flights and select the one that best suits your schedule and preferences.

7. Enter Passenger Details

  • Fill in details for all travelers in your booking.

8. Payment

  • Choose your payment method:
    • Credit Card
    • Debit Card
    • Netbanking
    • SkyPoints 24 loyalty points (for eligible members)
  • Complete the payment process to confirm your booking.

9. Booking Confirmation

  • Receive an instant booking confirmation via email and SMS.
  • Your itinerary will also be available in the “Manage My Flight” section.

Additional Features

Manage My Flight

  • Cancel your booking
  • Change your booking
  • Choose or change your seat
  • Upgrade your flight
  • Manage chauffeur-drive
  • Book accessible travel
  • Check-in online (or use other check-in options)

Contact & Support

For additional assistance, visit CloudWay24.com or use in-app support.
