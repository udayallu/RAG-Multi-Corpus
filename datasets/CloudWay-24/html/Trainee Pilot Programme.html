CloudWay 24 Trainee Pilot Programme

CloudWay 24 is committed to nurturing the next generation of world-class aviators through its prestigious Trainee Pilot Programme. Designed for passionate and driven individuals, our programme combines rigorous academic instruction, state-of-the-art simulator training, and hands-on flight experience with our modern fleet of Boeing and Airbus aircraft.

Programme Highlights

  • Comprehensive Training: Ground school, simulator training, and in-aircraft experience.
  • Fleet Exposure: Practical training on Boeing 787 Dreamliners, Airbus A350, and A380s.
  • Experienced Instructors: Learn from senior CloudWay 24 captains and industry experts.
  • Career Pathway: Successful trainees may join CloudWay 24 as Junior First Officers.
  • Stipend & Benefits: Competitive stipend during training, medical coverage, and travel benefits.

    • Passion for aviation and ability to work in a team.

Application Process

  1. Online Application: Fill out the form on CloudWay24.com
  2. Screening Test: Aptitude and psychometric assessments.
  3. Personal Interview: Panel interview with training and HR teams.
  4. Medical Examination: DGCA Class 1 Medical.
  5. Final Selection: Offer letter and induction into the training academy.
