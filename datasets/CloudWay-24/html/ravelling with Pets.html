Travelling with Pets on CloudWay 24

CloudWay 24 welcomes you and your furry companions aboard! To ensure a pleasant and safe journey for both you and your pet, please review our comprehensive guidelines for travelling with pets.

General Policy

  • Eligible Pets: Cats and small dogs (domesticated), in good health and at least 3 months old.
  • Allowed Routes: Pets are permitted only on international flights operated exclusively by CloudWay 24.
  • Advance Booking: Notify CloudWay 24 at least 48 hours before departure to reserve a spot for your pet.

Carrier Requirements

  • In-Cabin: Soft-sided, leak-proof, well-ventilated carrier that fits under the seat.
  • Checked: Rigid carrier with secure latch, absorbent bedding, and enough room for your pet to stand, turn, and lie down comfortably.
  • Identification tag (with pet name and contact info).
  • One carrier per passenger. Maximum 2 pets per booking (1 in-cabin, 1 in hold).

Health & Documentation

  • Valid health certificate from a licensed veterinarian issued within 10 days of departure.
  • Vaccination records (including rabies certificate).
  • Import/export permits, if required by destination country.

Check-In & Boarding

  • Arrive at least 2 hours before departure for check-in.
  • Use designated pet relief and holding areas (where available).
  • In-cabin pets must remain in their carrier throughout the flight—no exceptions.

Restrictions

  • No pets allowed in CloudLux (First Class) or on select aircraft types due to space limitations.
  • Brachycephalic breeds (flat-faced dogs/cats) may not be allowed in the hold for safety reasons.
  • Not permitted as cargo or unaccompanied (except registered service animals—see special needs policy).

How to Book

  1. During flight booking: Select “Travelling with Pets” and enter pet details.
  2. After booking: Use “Manage My Flight” > “Add a Pet” option or contact Customer Care.
  3. Payment: Pet travel fees must be paid online with Credit Card, Debit Card, Netbanking, or SkyPoints 24 points.

Tips for a Smooth Journey

  • Feed your pet a light meal 2 hours before departure.
  • Avoid tranquilizing your pet unless prescribed by a vet.
  • Place a familiar toy or blanket in the carrier for comfort.
  • Attach a current photo of your pet to the carrier.
