CloudWay 24 Baggage Allowance Policy

CloudWay 24 offers generous and convenient baggage allowances tailored to each cabin class, ensuring a comfortable and seamless journey for all passengers.

Cabin Baggage Allowance

Class Number of Bags Max Weight (per bag) Maximum Dimensions (L x W x H)
CloudLux (First) 2 10 kg each 55 x 40 x 23 cm
CloudElite (Business) 2 8 kg each 55 x 40 x 23 cm
CloudComfort (Premium Economy) 1 8 kg 55 x 40 x 23 cm
CloudSaver (Economy) 1 7 kg 55 x 40 x 23 cm

In addition: 1 small personal item (laptop bag/handbag) per passenger is permitted.

Checked Baggage Allowance

Class Number of Bags Max Weight (per bag) Total Allowance
CloudLux (First) 3 32 kg 96 kg
CloudElite (Business) 2 32 kg 64 kg
CloudComfort (Premium Economy) 2 23 kg 46 kg
CloudSaver (Economy) 1 23 kg 23 kg

Bags exceeding 158 cm (62 in) in total dimensions (L+W+H) or the permitted weight may incur excess baggage fees.

Excess Baggage Policy

  • Excess Baggage: Can be pre-purchased at a discounted rate via the CloudWay24 app or website during booking or under “Manage My Flight.”
  • At Airport: Higher fees apply for additional or overweight baggage checked at the airport counter.
  • SkyPoints 24 Members: Elite tier members may enjoy additional free allowance—check your SkyPoints 24 tier benefits.

Special Baggage

  • Sporting equipment and musical instruments accepted as part of checked baggage allowance; items exceeding size/weight limits may attract charges.
  • Contact customer service for special arrangements or detailed advice.

Important Guidelines

  • All baggage must be properly labeled with your name and contact information.
  • Carry-on luggage must fit in the overhead bin or under the seat.
  • Dangerous or prohibited items not allowed.

For the most up-to-date baggage rules and detailed FAQs, visit CloudWay24.com/Baggage or check the “Baggage Policy” section in the CloudWay24 app.
