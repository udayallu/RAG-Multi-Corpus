CloudWay 24: Visa and Passport Requirements Guide

Check Your Visa and Passport Requirements

Before booking your CloudWay 24 flight, ensure that you meet all entry requirements for your destination. Below is a quick-reference guide for visa and passport requirements for travelers from India to countries in Europe, Western, and Middle East regions.

General Requirements

  • Passport Validity: Minimum 6 months validity from your date of arrival in most international destinations.
  • Blank Pages: At least 2 blank pages recommended for visa/stamping.
  • Return Ticket: Proof of onward/return travel may be required.
  • Travel Insurance: Mandatory for Schengen (Europe) and recommended for others.

How to Check Your Requirements

  1. Visit CloudWay24.com or use the CloudWay24 App.
  2. Go to “Plan My Flight” – select your route and dates.
  3. Click on “Visa & Passport Info” on the booking page.
  4. Review requirements for your nationality, transit rules, and special advisories.

Disclaimer

  • Visa and entry rules change frequently.
  • Always verify the latest requirements on the destination embassy/consulate website or with your travel agent before departure.
