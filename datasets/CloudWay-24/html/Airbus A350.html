Airbus A350 – CloudWay 24 Fleet Overview

Aircraft Overview

The Airbus A350 is a next-generation, long-haul wide-body aircraft known for its advanced aerodynamics, fuel efficiency, and passenger comfort. Operated by CloudWay 24 on major intercontinental routes, the A350 delivers a premium flying experience across all cabin classes.

  • Aircraft Manufacturer: Airbus
  • Aircraft Model: A350-900
  • Total Seating Capacity: 320 passengers
