Web Check-In Guide – CloudWay 24

Web check-in with CloudWay 24 is an easy and convenient way to confirm your travel, choose your seat, and save valuable time at the airport. Here’s everything you need to know:

What is Web Check-In?

Web check-in allows you to check-in for your CloudWay 24 flight online via the website or mobile app before arriving at the airport. You can:

  • Select/modify your seat
  • Download/print your boarding pass
  • Add frequent flyer details
  • Save time and avoid airport queues

When Can I Check-In Online?

  • Opens: 48 hours before scheduled departure
  • Closes: 2 hours before the flight departure

How to Web Check-In with CloudWay 24

Step 1: Access Web Check-In

  • Visit CloudWay24.com OR open the CloudWay24 mobile app
  • Go to: Manage My Flight → Web Check-In

Step 2: Enter Your Details

  • Provide your:
    • Booking Reference (PNR)
    • Last Name or Registered Mobile/Email

Step 3: Select Passengers

  • Choose the passenger(s) you wish to check-in from your booking.

Step 4: Choose Your Seat

  • View seat map and:
    • Select or upgrade your seat (upgrade charges apply for premium cabins)
    • Confirm seat selection

Step 5: Add Extras (Optional)

  • Indicate meal preferences
  • Add or update frequent flyer details
  • Request special assistance

Step 6: Confirm & Get Boarding Pass

  • Confirm your check-in details
  • Receive your boarding pass:
    • Download as PDF
    • Get a mobile e-boarding pass
    • Option to have it emailed

Baggage Drop at the Airport

  • Already checked-in online?
    Drop off check-in bags at the CloudWay 24 Baggage Drop Counter at least 60 minutes before departure.

  • Traveling with only cabin baggage?
    Proceed directly to security with your e-boarding pass and valid ID.

Important Notes

  • Web check-in may not be available for:
    • Passengers needing special assistance (medical, minors traveling alone, etc.)
    • Certain international flights due to security regulations
  • Valid photo identification is mandatory at the airport, along with your boarding pass.
  • If you have upgraded or changed your booking after check-in, re-issue your boarding pass.
