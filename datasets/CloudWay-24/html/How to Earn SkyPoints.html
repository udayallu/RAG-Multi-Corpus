How to Earn SkyPoints 24 with CloudWay 24

SkyPoints 24 is CloudWay 24’s exclusive frequent flyer program, designed to reward loyal travelers with points on every journey and offer exclusive benefits.

Ways to Earn SkyPoints 24

1. Flight Bookings

  • Earn SkyPoints 24 every time you fly with CloudWay 24:
    • Points are credited based on distance flown, class of travel, and SkyPoints 24 tier.
    • All cabin classes qualify:
      • CloudLux (First Class) – Highest points accrual
      • CloudElite (Business Class)
      • CloudComfort (Premium Economy)
      • CloudSaver (Economy)
  • How points are calculated:
    • Base Points per mile/kilometer, with bonus for higher cabin class and tier status.

2. Booking Upgrades

  • Upgrade using cash or SkyPoints 24 and earn points on the original and upgraded booking value.

3. Partner Flights

  • Earn SkyPoints 24 on partner airline flights:
    • Book with CloudWay 24’s code-share and alliance partners using your SkyPoints 24 membership number.
    • Points accrual depends on the fare class and partner agreement.

4. Hotel & Car Rental Partners

  • Book with CloudWay 24 hotel or rental car partners through CloudWay24.com or app:
    • Add your SkyPoints 24 number when booking to earn points for eligible stays/rentals.

5. Shopping & Lifestyle Partners

  • Earn points by shopping with CloudWay 24 retail, dining, and lifestyle partners:
    • See the latest partner list and eligible offers on CloudWay24.com or the app.

6. Special Promotions

  • Participate in exclusive promotions:
    • Bonus SkyPoints 24 on select routes, during festive periods, or for milestone journeys.
    • Check “Offers & Promotions” on the website/app or your registered email for details.

How to Maximize Points

  • Book directly: Reserve flights via CloudWay24.com or the official app for full points.
  • Choose higher cabins: Higher fares (CloudLux, CloudElite) yield more points.
  • Link your SkyPoints 24 number: Always provide your membership number for every eligible booking and purchase.
  • Look out for double/mileage bonus promotions!

Track Your Points

  • Access your account on CloudWay24.com or the CloudWay24 app
  • Check “My SkyPoints 24” dashboard for:
    • Earned, bonus, and pending points balance
    • Recent transactions and activity history

Important Notes

  • Points eligibility: Only qualifying fares and eligible partners earn SkyPoints 24.
  • Retro-claim: Missed points? Submit a retro-claim via the SkyPoints 24 section within 6 months of your travel/purchase.
  • Terms Apply: Please refer to the detailed SkyPoints 24 program T&Cs at CloudWay24.com.
