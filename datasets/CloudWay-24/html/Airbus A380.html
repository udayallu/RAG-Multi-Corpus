Airbus A380: Overview & Cabin Features

Aircraft Overview: Airbus A380

The Airbus A380 is the world’s largest passenger airliner, renowned for its double-deck, wide-body design, offering unmatched space, comfort, and quietness in long-haul travel. With its advanced aerodynamics and efficient engines, the A380 is a flagship for many leading airlines, optimizing both capacity and passenger experience.

Seating Capacity

  • Total Seating: Typically 500–575 passengers (can be configured for up to 853 in all-economy layout)
  • Decks: Double-deck (Main and Upper)
  • Class Configuration (typical 4-class layout):
    • First Class: 8–14 seats
    • Business Class: 70–100 seats
    • Premium Economy: 50–55 seats
    • Economy Class: 350–450 seats

Cabin Features by Class

1. First Class (CloudLux)

  • Seating: Fully flat suites with privacy doors (1-2-1 configuration)
  • Features:
    • Personal suite with lie-flat bed
    • Direct aisle access
    • Luxury amenity kits & designer bedding
    • Personal wardrobe storage
    • Private minibar
    • In-seat power and USB charging
    • 32-40” HD inflight entertainment screens
    • Access to onboard lounge/bar (select airlines)
    • Fine-dining menus, served on demand

2. Business Class (CloudElite)

  • Seating: Lie-flat seats, staggered or herringbone layout (1-2-1 or 2-2-2)
  • Features:
    • 180° lie-flat seats with direct aisle access
    • Plush bedding and privacy dividers
    • On-demand gourmet dining
    • 18-24” personal entertainment screens
    • Ample storage & power outlets
    • Enhanced amenity kits
    • Access to onboard lounge (on select aircraft)

3. Premium Economy (CloudComfort)

  • Seating: Wider seats, extra legroom (2-3-2 or 2-4-2)
  • Features:
    • Up to 38” seat pitch, 19–20” width
    • Deeper seat recline and adjustable footrests
    • Enhanced meal service & welcome drinks
    • 13–15” personal screens
    • Dedicated cabin and lavatories
    • Extra luggage allowance

4. Economy Class (CloudSaver)

  • Seating: Standard seats (3-4-3 on main deck; 2-4-2 on upper deck, select rows)
  • Features:
    • Seat pitch: 31–33”, width: 18–18.5”
    • Adjustable headrests
    • Personal 10–13” HD screens
    • Hundreds of movies, music, games
    • Complimentary meals and beverages
    • Family and bassinet seating options
    • Power outlets on select fleets

Additional A380 Features

  • Spacious cabins with reduced noise levels
  • Mood lighting for reduced jetlag
  • Wide aisles and larger lavatories
  • Onboard bars/lounges for premium cabins (some airlines)
  • Advanced air filtration and humidity control
  • Wheelchair-accessible lavatories

Note: Seating configuration and amenities may vary by airline.
