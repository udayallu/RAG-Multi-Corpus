Boarding Process with CloudWay 24

CloudWay 24 is committed to delivering a smooth and efficient boarding experience for all passengers. Below is an outline of the standard boarding process, from check-in to takeoff.

1. Check-In

  • Online Check-In:
    Available 48 to 2 hours prior to departure via CloudWay24.com or the CloudWay 24 app (iOS & Android).
  • Airport Check-In Counters:
    Open 3 hours before international departures and close 60 minutes prior to departure.
  • Required Documents:
    Passport, visa (if required), and valid ticket/boarding pass.

2. Baggage Drop

  • Proceed to the designated baggage drop counter for your class:
    • CloudLux / CloudElite: Dedicated Priority Counters
    • CloudComfort / CloudSaver: Standard Counters
  • Ensure all bags abide by CloudWay 24’s baggage policy (refer to ticket for details).

3. Security & Immigration

  • After check-in, proceed to security and immigration with boarding pass and travel documents.
  • Allow additional time for security checks during peak hours.

4. Proceed to Gate

  • Check your boarding gate on your boarding pass, the airport displays, or the CloudWay 24 app.
  • Boarding gates typically open 60 minutes and close 20 minutes before departure.
  • Priority boarding is announced and available for:
    • CloudLux (First Class)
    • CloudElite (Business Class)
    • SkyPoints 24 Elite/Frequent Flyers
    • Passengers requiring special assistance and families with infants

5. Boarding Sequence

  1. Pre-Boarding:
    Passengers who need special assistance, unaccompanied minors, and families with small children.
  2. Priority Boarding:
    CloudLux, CloudElite, and eligible SkyPoints 24 members.
  3. Zone Boarding:
    Remaining passengers board by zone, as indicated on the boarding pass or by gate staff.
  4. Final Call:
    Last chance to board; listen for announcements.

6. During Boarding

  • Present your boarding pass and identification at the gate.
  • Gate staff will scan and verify your documents.
  • Follow signs and crew guidance to your designated seat.
  • Cabin crew will assist with stowing carry-on luggage and provide information as needed.

7. Departure

  • Please be seated and fasten your seatbelt prior to door closure.
  • Cabin crew will conduct safety demonstrations and perform final checks.
  • Relax and prepare for takeoff — your CloudWay 24 journey is about to begin!

Tips for Smooth Boarding

  • Download and use the CloudWay 24 App for real-time updates, mobile boarding passes, and gate changes.
  • Arrive at the airport well in advance, especially for international travel.
  • Have all travel documents ready for inspection at each stage.

For additional queries or assistance, visit CloudWay24.com or contact our customer support.
