Boeing 787 Dreamliner – CloudWay 24 Fleet Overview

CloudWay 24 operates a modern fleet of Boeing 787 Dreamliners, renowned for their fuel efficiency, passenger comfort, and advanced technology. Below is an overview of the aircraft’s seating capacity and detailed breakdown of class-wise cabin features as configured for CloudWay 24’s international long-haul service.

Aircraft: Boeing 787 Dreamliner

  • Total Seating Capacity: 290 seats (Typical CloudWay 24 configuration)

Cabin Class & Seating Configuration

Cabin Class No. of Seats Configuration
CloudLux 8 1-2-1
CloudElite 32 1-2-1
CloudComfort 40 2-3-2
CloudSaver 210 3-3-3

Cabin Class Features

1. CloudLux (First Class)

  • Seating: 180° lie-flat suites with privacy doors
  • Amenities: Personal wardrobe, luxury amenity kit, plush duvet & pillow
  • Entertainment: 22-inch HD screen, noise-cancelling headphones
  • Dining: Gourmet à la carte dining, exclusive fine wines & spirits
  • Other: Priority boarding, chauffeur service (select routes), access to CloudWay 24 First Lounge

2. CloudElite (Business Class)

  • Seating: Full-flat beds, direct aisle access
  • Amenities: Premium bedding, enhanced amenity kit
  • Entertainment: 18-inch HD touch screen, premium headphones
  • Dining: Multi-course menu, curated beverage selection
  • Other: Priority check-in, lounge access

3. CloudComfort (Premium Economy)

  • Seating: Wider seats, extra legroom (38” pitch), deeper recline
  • Amenities: Amenity kit, plush blanket & pillow
  • Entertainment: 13-inch personal screen
  • Dining: Upgraded meal selection, complimentary beverage service
  • Other: Priority boarding

4. CloudSaver (Economy)

  • Seating: Ergonomically-designed seats (31–32” pitch)
  • Amenities: Standard bedding, basic amenity kit on long-haul
  • Entertainment: 10-inch personal screen with on-demand movies, TV, games
  • Dining: Complimentary meals & snacks, beverage service

Additional Onboard Features

  • High-speed Wi-Fi connectivity (paid and complimentary plans by class)
  • USB and universal power outlets in all cabins
  • Mood lighting and improved cabin air quality
  • Extra-large, dimmable windows
