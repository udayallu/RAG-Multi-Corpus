# CloudWay 24 Dangerous Goods Policy

As per international aviation safety regulations and CloudWay 24's commitment to passenger safety, certain items are classified as **Dangerous Goods** and are strictly regulated. Please review the following policy to ensure compliance and avoid inconvenience during travel.

---

## General Guidelines

- **Prohibited Items:** Items that present a risk to health, safety, property, or the environment are **strictly prohibited**.
- **Declaration:** All dangerous goods must be declared at the time of check-in.
- **Special Permission:** Some items may be allowed only with prior approval.

---

## Important Notes

- **Liquids, Gels, Aerosols:** In hand baggage, each container must be 100ml or less; total may not exceed 1 litre per passenger (packed in a clear, resealable bag).
- **Electronics:** Lithium battery-operated equipment may be carried in both check-in and hand baggage, but spares must only be in hand baggage and protected from short-circuiting.
- **Sharp Items:** Prohibited in hand baggage. Limited sizes and types may go in checked baggage if securely packed.
