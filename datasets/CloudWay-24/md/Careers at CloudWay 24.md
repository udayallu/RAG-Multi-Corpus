##  Current Openings: Flight Operations

### 1. **Trainee Pilot**
- **Working Hours:** As per flying roster; typically, flight duty periods with adequate rest per DGCA/EASA/FAA regulations.
- **Location:** Mumbai, Delhi, Bengaluru (assigned base after selection)
- **Educational Qualifications:**
  - Minimum: 10+2 (Physics & Mathematics mandatory)
  - Valid Commercial Pilot License (CPL) issued by DGCA/ICAO member state
  - English Language Proficiency: Level 4 or above
- **Package:** INR 15-18 lakhs per annum + benefits
- **Roles & Responsibilities:**
  - Undergo training on Boeing 787/Airbus A350/A380 as allocated.
  - Assist in pre-flight checks, planning and cockpit preparation.
  - Comply with all SOPs, safety, and regulatory standards.
  - Develop and maintain proficiency through simulator and classroom training.
  - Shadow certified pilots and support flight operations.

---

### 2. **First Officer**
- **Working Hours:** Roster-based flying shifts; as per regulatory limits and airline scheduling.
- **Location:** Mumbai, Delhi, Bengaluru (assigned base)
- **Educational Qualifications:**
  - Valid ATPL or Frozen ATPL
  - Minimum total flying hours as specified by DGCA/ICAO for type
  - Current type rating (Boeing 787 Dreamliner / Airbus A350 / Airbus A380)
- **Package:** INR 38-48 lakhs per annum + bonuses & benefits
- **Roles & Responsibilities:**
  - Operate flights under Captain’s command.
  - Assist Captain in safe aircraft operation, navigation, and communication.
  - Execute flight duties as per CloudWay 24 and international standards.
  - Participate in recurrent training and skill assessments.

---

### 3. **Senior First Officer**
- **Working Hours:** Flight duty as per roster, including long-haul sectors.
- **Location:** Mumbai, Delhi, Bengaluru (assigned base)
- **Educational Qualifications:**
  - Valid ATPL
  - Significant experience as First Officer (min. 2000+ hours, at least 1000 hours on type preferred)
  - Current type rating (B787/A350/A380)
- **Package:** INR 59-65 lakhs per annum + variable components
- **Roles & Responsibilities:**
  - Perform all First Officer duties with advanced responsibilities.
  - Act as PIC (where required) on select sectors.
  - Mentor and guide Trainee Pilots and First Officers.
  - Support Captain in flight management and operational decision-making.

---

### 4. **Captain & Above**
- **Working Hours:** Variable, scheduled as per flight operations; includes command on ultra-long-haul flights.
- **Location:** Mumbai, Delhi, Bengaluru (assigned base)
- **Educational Qualifications:**
  - Valid ATPL with PIC rating on Boeing 787 / Airbus A350 / A380
  - Total flying experience: 5000+ hours (2000+ hours as PIC on type)

- **Package:** INR 1.1 - 1.5 crores per annum + performance incentives, travel perks & leadership benefits
- **Roles & Responsibilities:**
  - Command and supervise international flights ensuring utmost safety.
  - Decision making in all operational aspects, including emergencies.
  - Ensure compliance with all regulatory and company standards.
  - Lead and mentor cockpit and cabin crew.
  - Represent CloudWay 24 in audits and regulatory checks.

# Careers at CloudWay 24: Cabin Crew Recruitment

Are you ready to soar high and deliver world-class service above the clouds? Join the **CloudWay 24 Cabin Crew** and be an ambassador of seamless skies and global hospitality.

---

##  Job Title

**Cabin Crew Member**

---

## Working Hours

- **Roster-based Shifts:** 8–12 hours per shift, including weekends, holidays, and overnight layovers.
- **Flexible Schedules:** Aligned with international flight operations and layovers.

---

## Location

- **Primary Location:** Mumbai, India (Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj International Airport)
- **Additional Base Locations:** Delhi (Indira Gandhi International Airport) & Bengaluru (Kempegowda International Airport)
- International layovers based on operational requirements.

---

##  Educational Qualifications

- Minimum 10+2 (Higher Secondary) from a recognized board.
- Diploma / Degree in Hospitality, Travel, or Customer Service preferred (not mandatory).
- Proficiency in English (spoken & written). Additional languages are an advantage.

---

## Package & Benefits

- **Salary:** INR 6–8 lakhs per annum (starting, based on experience & location)
- **Allowances:** International layover allowance, flying allowance, and meal allowance.
- **Benefits:** Medical & life insurance, comprehensive training, annual leave, free/reduced flight tickets (for self & family), and access to CloudWay 24 Lounges.
- **Incentives:** Performance and service excellence bonuses.
- Membership in the exclusive **SkyPoints 24 Staff Rewards Program**.

---

## Roles & Responsibilities

- **Passenger Safety:** Ensure safety and security procedures are executed as per global aviation standards.
- **World-Class Service:** Deliver premium in-flight service in all cabin classes—CloudLux, CloudElite, CloudComfort, and CloudSaver.
- **Pre-Flight Checks:** Conduct safety checks before flight boarding.
- **Passenger Comfort:** Assist passengers with seating, stowage of baggage, and comfort-related requests.
- **Emergency Handling:** Professionally handle medical emergencies, turbulence, and evacuations if required.
- **Communication:** Make announcements and communicate information clearly and efficiently.
- **Team Collaboration:** Work harmoniously with the crew, flight deck, and ground teams.
- **Brand Representation:** Uphold and embody the CloudWay 24 values—excellence, empathy, and professionalism at all times.
