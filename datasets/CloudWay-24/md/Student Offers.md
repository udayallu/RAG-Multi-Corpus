# CloudWay 24 – Student Offers

CloudWay 24 recognizes the unique travel needs of students pursuing education or attending academic events across the globe. Our exclusive **Student Offers** provide valuable benefits and flexibility, making international journeys more affordable and comfortable!

---

## **Student Offer Highlights**

- **Up to 10% Discount**  
  Enjoy special fares with up to 10% off* on CloudSaver (Economy) and CloudComfort (Premium Economy) fares for students aged 18–30.

- **Extra Baggage Allowance**  
  Travel worry-free! Students get **1 additional checked bag (up to 23kg)** on all international routes.

- **Flexible Date Change**  
  One-time free date change** in case your travel plans or academic schedule changes.

- **Priority Check-In**  
  Skip the queue with dedicated student check-in counters at major CloudWay 24 hubs.

- **SkyPoints 24 Bonus**  
  Earn **500 bonus SkyPoints 24** for your first student booking.

---

## **Who Can Avail?**

- Students aged 18–30 years
- Possessing a valid student ID or university admission/offer letter
- Bookings for outbound or inbound international flights from India

---

## **How to Book the Student Offer?**

1. **Sign Up/Login**  
   Register on [CloudWay24.com](https://CloudWay24.com) or the CloudWay24 App  
   (Email or mobile verification required)

2. **Plan Your Flight**  
   Select “Student Fare” when booking.  
   Choose your route, travel dates, and cabin class.

3. **Upload/Verify Student ID**  
   At checkout, upload a valid student ID/admission letter for verification.

4. **Pay Securely**  
   Choose your preferred payment option:  
   - Credit Card  
   - Debit Card  
   - Netbanking  
   - SkyPoints 24 points

---

## **Manage Your Student Booking**

Go to **“Manage My Flight”** (via web/app):  
- Modify or cancel your booking  
- Change seats or upgrade
- Web check-in

---

## **Terms & Conditions**

- *Up to 10% discount applicable only on base fare; taxes and surcharges apply.
- **One-time free date change permitted up to 48 hours before scheduled departure.
- Additional baggage allowed only upon presenting proof at check-in desk.
- Offer subject to seat availability and black-out dates (public holidays, peak seasons).
- CloudWay 24 reserves the right to withdraw/modify the offer at any time.
