# CloudWay 24: Employee Insurance Policy Overview

CloudWay 24 provides comprehensive group insurance coverage for all employees, ensuring their health, safety, and well-being while on duty and beyond. Insurance benefits are categorized based on employee roles due to the varying risk profiles associated with their responsibilities.

## Insurance Policy Categories

### 1. Non-Ground Staff
**(Pilots & Cabin Crew)**

- **Nature of Coverage:**  
  Enhanced coverage due to higher occupational risk (airborne duties, international layovers, irregular schedules).

- **Policy Limit Highlights:**
    - **Group Life Insurance:** INR 1.5 Crore (approx. $180,000)
    - **Accidental Death & Disability:** INR 2 Crore (approx. $240,000)
    - **Medical Insurance (per annum):** INR 10 Lakh (~$12,000)
    - **Critical Illness Cover:** INR 20 Lakh (~$24,000)
    - **Loss of License Benefit:** Applicable to Pilots (up to INR 1 Crore/$120,000 for loss of medical certification)
    - **Travel Insurance:** Comprehensive, global coverage included

---

### 2. Ground Staff
**(Engineering, HR, Administration, Support Services, and Other Employees)**

- **Nature of Coverage:**  
  Standard coverage reflecting relatively lower occupational exposure.

- **Policy Limit Highlights:**
    - **Group Life Insurance:** INR 50 Lakh (approx. $60,000)
    - **Accidental Death & Disability:** INR 75 Lakh (approx. $90,000)
    - **Medical Insurance (per annum):** INR 5 Lakh (~$6,000)
    - **Critical Illness Cover:** INR 10 Lakh (~$12,000)
    - **Loss of License Benefit:** Not Applicable
    - **Travel Insurance:** Domestic and limited international work travel coverage

---

## Additional Notes

- **Family Coverage:**  
  Medical insurance can be extended to spouse and dependent children at preferential group rates.

- **Claim Settlement:**  
  All claims are facilitated by the Corporate HR and Employee Well-being Department in partnership with leading insurance providers.

- **Review & Update:**  
  Insurance limits and coverage are reviewed annually as per regulatory standards and operational requirements.
