# Airbus A350 – CloudWay 24 Fleet Overview

## Aircraft Overview
The **Airbus A350** is a next-generation, long-haul wide-body aircraft known for its advanced aerodynamics, fuel efficiency, and passenger comfort. Operated by CloudWay 24 on major intercontinental routes, the A350 delivers a premium flying experience across all cabin classes.

- **Aircraft Manufacturer:** Airbus
- **Aircraft Model:** A350-900
- **Total Seating Capacity:** 320 passengers

---

## Class-wise Seating Capacity & Cabin Features

### 1. **CloudLux (First Class)**
- **Seats:** 8
- **Configuration:** 1-2-1
- **Key Features:**
  - Fully enclosed private suites
  - Lie-flat beds with luxury bedding
  - Personal 21” HD touchscreen entertainment system
  - Gourmet on-demand dining, exclusive menu
  - Direct aisle access
  - Dedicated First Class lounge access
  - Personalized service with dedicated crew
  - Amenity kit by luxury brand

### 2. **CloudElite (Business Class)**
- **Seats:** 42
- **Configuration:** 1-2-1
- **Key Features:**
  - Lie-flat beds (78” pitch)
  - All seats with direct aisle access
  - 18” personal HD entertainment screen
  - Multi-course meals, fine wines & beverages
  - Ambient mood lighting
  - Noise-cancelling headphones
  - Enhanced storage space & privacy dividers
  - Priority check-in & boarding
  - Lounge access

### 3. **CloudComfort (Premium Economy)**
- **Seats:** 32
- **Configuration:** 2-4-2
- **Key Features:**
  - Wider seats with extra legroom (38” pitch)
  - Enhanced recline and footrests
  - 13” personal HD screen
  - Welcome drink & upgraded meal options
  - Power outlets & USB ports at every seat
  - Dedicated cabin crew

### 4. **CloudSaver (Economy Class)**
- **Seats:** 238
- **Configuration:** 3-3-3
- **Key Features:**
  - Ergonomically designed seats
  - 32” seat pitch, adjustable headrests
  - 11” personal touchscreen display
  - Complimentary meals & beverages
  - USB charging ports
  - In-flight WiFi (paid service)
  - Family seating options
