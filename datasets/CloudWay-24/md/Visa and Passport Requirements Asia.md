## Popular Asian Destinations: Visa Policy Overview

### 1. Singapore
- **Visa-Free:**  Indian citizens (up to 30 days for select categories, mostly require e-visa prior to travel)
- **E-Visa:**  Required for Indian passport holders (Apply before travel)
- **On Arrival:**  Not available
- **Documents:**  Proof of funds, return ticket, hotel booking

---

### 2. Thailand
- **Visa-On-Arrival:** For Indian citizens (up to 15 days, fee payable, limited entry points)
- **E-Visa:** Option available
- **Requirements:**  Proof of accommodation, funds, return ticket

---

### 3. Japan
- **Visa Required:** All Indian citizens must obtain a visa in advance from the Japanese embassy/consulate
- **Documents:**  Invitation letter/travel itinerary, bank statement, return ticket

---

### 4. Malaysia
- **E-Visa:** Available for Indian tourists (Apply online prior to travel, valid for 30 days)
- **Visa-On-Arrival:** Possible when arriving from Singapore, Thailand, Indonesia, or Brunei (conditions apply)
- **Requirements:** Proof of funds, return/onward ticket, hotel booking

---

### 5. United Arab Emirates (Dubai)
- **E-Visa:** Required for Indian passport holders (Apply online, typically via airline or sponsor)
- **Visa-on-arrival:** For Indian passport holders with US Green Card/UK/EU Residence Permit (14 days)
- **Documents:** Return ticket, hotel booking

---

### 6. Indonesia (Bali/Jakarta)
- **Visa-on-arrival:** Indian citizens eligible (USD fee, valid for 30 days, extendable)
- **E-Visa:** Pre-application possible
- **Requirements:** Return flight, hotel booking

---

### 7. Nepal
- **Visa-Free:** For Indian citizens (no visa required, must carry valid passport/election ID)
- **Entry Points:** Airports and designated land borders

---

### 8. Bangladesh
- **Visa Required:** Indian citizens need a visa; advance application required
- **Documents:** Travel itinerary, invitation (as applicable), hotel booking

---

### 9. Hong Kong (SAR)
- **Pre-arrival Online Registration:** Mandatory for Indian citizens (up to 14 days stay)
- **Visa Required:** For stays longer than 14 days, work, or study

---

### 10. China
- **Visa Required:** Indian passport holders must obtain a visa prior to travel
- **Application:** At designated Visa Application Centres
- **Documents:** Detailed itinerary, hotel booking, financial proof
