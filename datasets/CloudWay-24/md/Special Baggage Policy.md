# CloudWay 24 Special Baggage Policy

CloudWay 24 offers customized handling and allowances for special baggage items across all travel classes. Passengers are encouraged to review class-wise entitlements and guidelines for items such as sports equipment, musical instruments, hunting/sport rifles, and ammunition.

---

## 1. Eligible Special Baggage Categories

- **Sports Equipment**
  - Golf bags, skis, snowboards, bicycles, surfboards, cricket kits, etc.
- **Musical Instruments**
  - Guitars, violins, cellos, musical keyboards, etc.
- **Hunting & Sport Rifles**
  - Permitted only with valid licenses and prior airline approval.
- **Ammunition**
  - Only as checked baggage, under strict regulatory compliance.

---

## 2. Class-wise Special Baggage Allowance

| Cabin Class     | Free Special Baggage Allowance  | Additional Fees                | Priority Handling |
|-----------------|-------------------------------|-------------------------------|------------------|
| **CloudLux (First Class)**    | 2 special items included            | Additional items: Standard excess baggage fee | Yes|
| **CloudElite (Business Class)** | 1 special item included             | Additional items: Standard excess baggage fee | Yes|
| **CloudComfort (Premium Economy)** | No free inclusion                   | All items: Standard excess baggage fee         | No |
| **CloudSaver (Economy)**  | No free inclusion                   | All items: Standard excess baggage fee         | No |

> **Note:** Size and weight limits apply. Items above permitted weight/size may attract oversize/overweight charges.

---

## 3. Packing & Handling Guidelines

### Sports Equipment
- Should be securely packed and labeled.
- Oversized items (e.g. bicycles, surfboards) require advance notice.
- Fragile items: Passenger advised to insure against damage.

### Musical Instruments
- Small instruments (up to 115 cm/45 in and 7kg): Allowed as cabin baggage (subject to space and safety).
- Larger instruments: Must be checked-in; special packing recommended; advance notification required.
- For extra protection, a paid seat may be booked for larger valuable instruments (subject to availability).

### Hunting & Sport Rifles, Ammunition
- **Only permitted as checked baggage with:**
  - Valid firearm license(s) and documentation.
  - Prior notification (at least 72 hours before departure).
  - Approval at origin and destination as per local and international regulations.
- **Packing:** Unloaded, dismantled if possible, in rigid locked cases.
- **Ammunition:** Limited to 5 kg per passenger, packed separately from firearms, in manufacturer’s packaging.

---

## 4. Advance Arrangement & Approval

- **Advance notification is mandatory** for all firearms, ammunition, and oversized/fragile items.
- Submit requests via “Manage My Flight” or contact CloudWay 24 Customer Service.
- Confirmation required before travel.

---

## 5. Fees and Charges

- **Refer to the [CloudWay 24 Baggage Calculator](https://cloudway24.com/baggage) for specific rates.**
- Fees vary by route, item size/weight, and membership tier (SkyPoints 24 members may get fee waivers).

---

## 6. Exclusions and Restrictions

- No carriage of explosive or incendiary items.
- Restrictions applied for travel to/from certain destinations due to local laws.
- Airline reserves the right to refuse any special baggage not meeting safety, security, or compliance requirements.
