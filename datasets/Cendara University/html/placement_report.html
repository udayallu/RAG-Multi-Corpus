Cendara University Placement Report

Overview

Cendara University is committed to bridging academic excellence with meaningful career opportunities for its graduates. Our robust campus placement program leverages strong industry connections, comprehensive career services, and dedicated faculty mentorship to ensure that students from all academic backgrounds transition successfully into the workforce, further study, or entrepreneurial ventures.

Placement Process

  1. Registration: Eligible final-year students register with the Center for Career Development and submit CVs.
  2. Pre-Placement Training: Includes resume workshops, interview preparation, group discussions, and aptitude tests.
  3. Company Presentations: Employers visit campus (virtually or in-person) to describe roles and organizational culture.
  4. Application & Shortlisting: Students apply to open positions; employers shortlist candidates based on their criteria.
  5. Interviews & Assessments: Conducted on campus or offsite, followed by HR and technical interviews.
  6. Offer Rollout: Successful candidates receive offer letters; assistance provided in decision-making and negotiations.

Note: Specialized support is provided for international students, differently abled students, and those seeking opportunities in non-traditional sectors.
