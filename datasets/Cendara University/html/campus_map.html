6. Key Facilities & Points of Interest

  • Athletic & Recreation Center (ARC)
    • Location: South Grounds
    • Features: Indoor pool, basketball courts, cardio/weights, group fitness studios
  • Sylvan Library Rooftop Garden
    • Location: SLA, 4th Floor
    • Features: Outdoor study lounge, WiFi, green spaces
  • Fine Arts Amphitheatre
    • Location: East Promenade, behind LAM
    • Capacity: 250 seats, open-air
  • Reflection Lake
    • Location: Central Quad
    • Features: Walking paths, benches, annual Springfest site
  • Campus Shuttle Stop
    • Locations: Main Entrance (South Gate), North Crescent, Commerce Circle
    • Service: On-campus and off-campus routes (M-F, 7am–11pm)

7. Accessibility

All major buildings are wheelchair accessible with automatic doors and elevators. Accessible parking spaces are available in each parking lot and garage.

8. Campus Safety Resources

  • 24/7 Campus Security Office: Located in the Walker Administration Building, Suite 105.
  • Escort Service: Available 6pm–2am daily. Call (732) 555-4488.
  • Automated External Defibrillators (AEDs): Located in all major buildings, clearly marked.
