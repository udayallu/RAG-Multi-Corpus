Cendara University Undergraduate and Graduate Fee Structure

Cendara University is committed to providing a transparent overview of the expected costs associated with enrollment. The fee ranges outlined below are for the academic year 2024–2025 and apply to both domestic and international students. Actual charges may vary based on chosen program, housing selection, dining preference, and individual course loads.

1. Tuition

Tuition varies by degree level and academic program. The following ranges represent annual full-time enrollment (24–30 credits undergraduate, 18–24 credits graduate):

Program/Degree Tuition Range (Per Year)
Bachelor of Arts/Science/Engineering $24,000 – $29,000
Bachelor of Business Administration/Nursing $26,000 – $32,000
Bachelor of Education $23,500 – $26,500
Master of Arts/Science/Engineering $13,500 – $18,200
Master of Business Administration/Public Health $17,800 – $21,400
Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.) $16,500 – $23,000
Graduate Certificate Programs $9,800 – $12,500 (per certificate)
  • Part-Time Tuition: Calculated per credit hour, typically $900 – $1,200/credit (undergraduate); $1,050 – $1,500/credit (graduate)
  • International students: Add $2,000 – $5,000 annually for certain programs

2. Mandatory Fees

All enrolled students are assessed general fees in addition to tuition.

Fee Category Annual Range Description
Student Activities Fee $350 – $500 Supports clubs, programming, and student government
Technology Fee $250 – $400 Access to campus computing and online resources
Health Services Fee $175 – $350 On-campus health clinic, mental health support
Library Fee $90 – $140 Library resources and special collections
Registration Fee $70 – $120 Course registration and records management
New Student Orientation $120 – $200 One-time, for all first-time enrollees

3. Room (Housing)

Cendara University offers several on-campus residence options. Costs vary by building, room type, and amenities.

Housing Option Annual Range Notes
Traditional Double $5,400 – $6,800 Shared room, communal bathroom
Single (Private) $7,200 – $9,100 Private room, shared facilities
Suite-Style (2–4 students) $8,600 – $10,400 Shared suite, en-suite bathroom
Apartment (Upperclass/Grad only) $9,800 – $12,250 Kitchen, living room, private BR
  • Utilities and internet included in all campus housing
  • Limited off-campus housing listings available ($450 – $950/month per person)

4. Board (Meal Plans)

Meal plans are required for first- and second-year residents.

Plan Type Annual Range Average Meals per Week
Platinum $5,200 – $5,850 Unlimited dining hall + retail credit
Gold $4,600 – $5,100 14 meals/week + snack credits
Silver $3,800 – $4,350 10 meals/week + à la carte access
  • Graduate and apartment residents may opt out or select partial plans ($700 – $2,200/year)

7. Important Notes

  • Scholarships, grants, and financial aid are available; contact the Office of Financial Assistance for eligibility.
  • Some specialized programs (e.g., Nursing, Engineering, Study Abroad) may charge additional instructional or experiential fees.
  • All fees are subject to annual review and adjustment by the Board of Trustees.
  • Billing is conducted on a semester basis; flexible payment plans are available upon request.
  • Questions about campus fees should be addressed to bursar@cendara.edu.

For complete policy details, including payment deadlines and refund schedules, please refer to the Cendara University Student Accounts Handbook.
