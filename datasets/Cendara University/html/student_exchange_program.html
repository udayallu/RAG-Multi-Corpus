2. Student Exchange Programs

Cendara University’s Office of International Affairs (OIA) manages a diverse portfolio of exchange opportunities for both inbound and outbound students.

A. Outbound Exchange Programs

Semester & Year-Long Exchanges

  • Eligibility: Fully enrolled undergraduate or graduate students, minimum GPA 3.0, language proficiency as required by host.
  • Destinations (2024/25 academic year):
    • Humboldt School of Technology, Berlin (Engineering)
    • Royal Institute of Social Sciences, London (Political Science & Sociology)
    • Université de la Loire, Tours (French Studies, Philosophy)
    • Victoria State University, Melbourne (Environmental Sciences, Marine Biology)

Summer Programs

  • East Asia Cultural Immersion (Seoul, Tokyo): 6-week program featuring language courses, company visits, and cultural excursions.
  • Pontifica Universidad del Sur (Santiago): 4-week Sustainability and Community Action project.

B. Inbound Exchange Opportunities

Cendara University welcomes over 70 exchange students annually from partner institutions. The following services are provided:

  • Pre-arrival orientation
  • Dedicated exchange student housing in Sterling Residence Hall
  • Academic advising and language support
  • Peer mentorship and local culture introduction

3. Faculty & Researcher Exchange

Faculty and research staff at Cendara University have access to:

  • Sabbatical Placement: Up to 2 semesters at Université de la Loire (Humanities Faculty) or Humboldt School of Technology (STEM departments).
  • Research Collaboration Grants: Joint funding with partner institutions for bi-annual themed workshops and collaborative research, e.g., the Royal Institute of Social Sciences’ annual Urban Studies Colloquium.
  • Teaching Fellowships: Visiting faculty positions at East Asia University Consortium with curriculum development roles and teaching assignments.

5. How to Apply

For Cendara University Students:

  • Attend a mandatory Exchange Program Information Session (monthly, OIA Conference Room).
  • Consult with academic advisors for course and credit transfer planning.
  • Submit complete application and supporting materials (transcript, personal statement, recommendation letter) by March 1 (Fall) or October 1 (Spring).

For Inbound Students:

  • Must be nominated by partner institution coordinator.
  • Submit online application via Cendara University OIA Portal.
  • Provide proof of English proficiency (TOEFL iBT ≥ 80 or IELTS ≥ 6.0 unless enrolled in English-medium program).

For Faculty/Researchers:

  • Contact departmental international liaison for available placements.
  • Prepare research proposal or teaching plan.
  • Attach CV and evidence of institutional support from home university.
