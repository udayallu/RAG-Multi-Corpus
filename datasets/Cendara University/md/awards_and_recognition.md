### 2023–2024 University Honors

#### Chancellor’s Award for Excellence in Teaching  
**Recipient:** Dr. Simone L. Marquez  
**Department:** Mathematics  
**Presented on:** May 4, 2024  
**Citation:**  
Dr. Marquez is recognized for distinguished teaching and innovative curriculum development in applied mathematics. Her engaging approach and dedication to student mentorship have significantly improved departmental outcomes.

---

#### Distinguished Alumni Achievement Award  
**Recipient:** Dr. Priyanka Halson (Class of 2005)  
**Field:** Medical Research  
**Presented on:** March 15, 2024  
**Citation:**  
Dr. Halson, Director of Cardiology at Meridian General Hospital, has pioneered advancements in heart disease diagnostics, receiving international acclaim. Her commitment to global health advocacy exemplifies Cendara values.

---

#### Staff Service Recognition  
**Recipient:** Mr. James O. Felix  
**Department:** Facilities & Operations  
**Years of Service:** 25  
**Presented on:** December 10, 2023  
**Citation:**  
Mr. Felix’s long-standing dedication has been vital to campus safety and sustainability initiatives. His efforts have contributed to Cendara attaining a regional Green Campus certification.

---

#### Undergraduate Research Excellence Award  
**Recipient:** Maya Elise Greene  
**Major:** Environmental Science  
**Presented on:** May 2, 2024  
**Citation:**  
Maya’s project on river pollution and restoration in the Cendara Valley garnered publication in the Northeast Ecology Review and shaped local environmental policy recommendations.

### 2023 Institutional Achievements

- **Top Regional University Award**  
  Granted by the Eastern Academic Review Board for outstanding academic innovation and student satisfaction.

- **Excellence in Diversity and Inclusion (Gold Level)**  
  Awarded by the National Association for Inclusive Education (NAIE) for Cendara’s “Bridge Horizons” initiative, boosting underrepresented groups in STEM by 35%.

- **Innovative Campus Sustainability Program Award**  
  Recognized by the Green Futures Alliance for the comprehensive “Solar Steps” campus energy overhaul, including solar canopy installations and electric fleet conversion.

  Team AquaAdapt (Katherine Su, Omar Bedeir, Linh Phan) reached the finals for their development of an affordable portable water purifier, April 2024.
