# Cendara University Accreditation Status

## Overview

Cendara University is a fully accredited higher education institution recognized nationally and internationally for its commitment to academic excellence, research innovation, and student support. Below is a summary of the university’s current accreditation status, accrediting bodies, certification levels, and institutional recognition.

---

## 1. Regional Accreditation

### Accrediting Body

- **Midland Commission on Colleges and Universities (MCCU)**
    - Accredited since: August 15, 1968
    - Most recent reaffirmation visit: March 2022
    - Accreditation valid through: June 2028

### Certification Level

- **Level VI** (Highest level for doctoral-granting institutions)

### Scope

- Institutional accreditation covering all undergraduate, graduate, and professional degree programs offered by Cendara University at all campus locations.

### Evaluation Comments (2022 Report)

- “Cendara University demonstrates sustained excellence in academic rigor, faculty qualifications, and student-centered support. The Commission commends the university’s commitment to continuous improvement and community engagement.”

## 3. State and Federal Recognition

- **State of Eddington Department of Higher Education**
    - Cendara University is licensed under the State of Eddington Higher Education Act, Section 312.
    - Current registration number: EDU-340182

- **U.S. Department of Education Recognition**
    - Listed as an Eligible Institution for Federal Student Aid (Title IV)
    - OPE ID: 00721800
    - Updated status: Compliant as of January 2024

## 5. Recent Accreditation Milestones
