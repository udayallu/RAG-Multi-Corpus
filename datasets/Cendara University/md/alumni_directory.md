## Alumni Networking Resources

Cendara University fosters lifelong connections through a variety of exclusive resources available to alumni and current students:

### Cendara Alumni Portal

- **Website:** [alumni.cendara.edu](https://alumni.cendara.edu)  
- Secure directory for searching graduates by city, discipline, or profession
- Profile updates and career achievements sharing

### Regional Alumni Chapters

- **Cendara Bay Area Club**: Quarterly mixers and tech seminars in San Francisco
- **Cendara London Society**: Cultural evenings and guest speaker panels
- **Southeast Asia Alumni Network**: Annual leadership summit in Kuala Lumpur

### Career Networking Events

- **Annual Career Connect** (April): Alumni and student panel discussions, resume workshops, job fair
- **Mentorship Week** (September): Alumni pairing with final-year students for career advice
- **Webinar Series:** Monthly online forums on topics ranging from entrepreneurship to nonprofit leadership

### Professional Interest Groups

- **Women in STEM Network:** Supporting gender equity in science and engineering
- **Cendara Entrepreneurs Collective:** Start-up networking and resource sharing
- **Arts & Letters Circle:** Alumni in creative fields meet for virtual and live salons

## Connecting Current Students with Alumni

Students are encouraged to:

- **Register for Alumni Mentors:** Sign up at [mentoring.cendara.edu](https://mentoring.cendara.edu)  
- **Attend Networking Nights:** Scheduled monthly in the Student Union Ballroom  
- **Access the Online Directory:** Contact alumni for informational interviews and internship opportunities
