Corporate & Government Discounts Policy (Raw Draft)

1. Corporate Discounts

Eligibility

  • Available to employees of approved corporate entities/partners possessing a valid company ID.
  • Bulk purchase schemes for corporate fleet requirements; eligibility as per agreement/MOU.

Discount Structure

  • General Corporate Discount:
    2% - 5% off on ex-showroom price (varying by model, quantity, and corporate tie-up).
  • Fleet Purchase Discount:
    5% - 8% off for purchase of 5+ vehicles in a single order.
  • Additional Benefits:
    • Free 1st year maintenance package.
    • Complimentary accessories kit on select models.
    • Priority delivery slots.

Documents Required

  • Valid Corporate ID Proof / Letter from HR or Procurement Head.
  • Company-issued Purchase Order (PO) or undertaking.
  • Employee ID Proof (for individual employee schemes).

Terms & Conditions

  • Corporate discounts cannot be clubbed with some seasonal offers, promotional rebates, or exchange bonuses.
  • Discount amount and benefits at discretion of Aventro Motors Pvt. Ltd. and can vary by region/dealership.
  • GST & other statutory levies payable on discounted price.

2. Government Discounts

Eligibility

  • Applicable to Central/State Government organizations, PSUs, Public Sector Banks, Municipal Corporations, and accredited government officials.
  • Available for both official and personal purchases (subject to organization’s rules).

Discount Structure

  • Direct Government Order:
    3% - 7% off on ex-showroom price.
  • Public Sector Employees:
    2% - 5% off on ex-showroom price (on submission of valid official ID).
  • Defence/Police/Paramilitary Personnel:
    Additional ₹10,000 - ₹20,000 benefit (model-specific).

Additional Perks

  • Priority processing for order and registration.
  • Fast-track delivery support.
  • Complimentary basic kit.

Documents Required

  • Official Purchase Order or Sanction Letter (institutional purchase).
  • Valid Government photo ID/Service Card (for personal purchase).
  • Departmental recommendation letter (if needed).

Terms & Conditions

  • Discounts subject to availability of models/variants and as per prevailing policy.
  • Not valid for resale prior to 1 year from delivery (especially for institutional sales).
  • Cannot be combined with some private exchange/finance offers.
  • Final discretion rests with Aventro Motors Pvt. Ltd.
