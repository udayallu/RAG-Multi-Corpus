Parts List & Industry Standard Prices

Below are typical details and industry average prices (2024) for commonly replaced automotive parts, applicable to Aventro as well as most mainstream passenger vehicles in India. Prices may vary by make/model, specification, and market.

1. Air Filter

  • Description: Filters airborne particles from entering the engine, crucial for clean air intake and engine health.
  • Average Market Price: ₹500 – ₹1,200

2. Oil Filter

  • Description: Removes contaminants from engine oil to protect engine internals and maintain lubrication quality.
  • Average Market Price: ₹300 – ₹800

3. Fuel Injector

  • Description: Delivers fuel to engine cylinders in precise amounts for efficient combustion.
  • Average Market Price: ₹2,000 – ₹6,000 (per injector)
  • Note: Most vehicles have 3–4 injectors (petrol); diesels may have more and be higher-priced.

4. Spark Plug

  • Description: Initiates ignition of the fuel-air mixture in petrol engines.
  • Average Market Price: ₹200 – ₹600 (per spark plug)
  • Note: Number required depends on engine (typically 3–4 for small cars, up to 6–8 for high-end/premium).

5. Alloy Wheels

  • Description: Lightweight, stylish wheels improving aesthetics and sometimes performance.
  • Average Market Price: ₹4,000 – ₹10,000 (per wheel; depends on size 13”–17” and design)
  • Note: Premium or OEM alloys for SUVs and sedans can cost more.

6. Headlamps

  • Description: Main front lights for visibility and road safety; may be halogen, LED, or projector types.
  • Average Market Price:
    • Halogen: ₹2,000 – ₹4,500 (per unit)
    • LED/Projector: ₹5,000 – ₹15,000 (per unit)
  • Note: Price varies by technology and vehicle segment.

7. Tail Lamps

  • Description: Rear light assemblies including brake, indicator, and reversing lights.
  • Average Market Price: ₹1,500 – ₹5,000 (per unit)
  • Note: LED tail lamp units and assembly with dynamic indicators may cost more.

Disclaimer:
- All prices above are approximate (as of 2024) and for standard parts.
- Prices are subject to GST and labor charges may be extra.
- For Aventro OEM pricing or specific vehicle compatibility, please contact your nearest Aventro Service Center or refer to your vehicle manual.
