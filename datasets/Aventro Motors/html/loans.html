Easy Car Loan Process

  1. Select Your Car
    Choose your favorite Aventro Motors model and variant at our showroom or website.

  2. Apply for Loan

    • Fill in a simple online/offline loan application form at the dealership or on our website.
    • Provide basic documents (ID, address proof, income proof, bank statement).

  3. Choose Loan Partner

    • Our finance advisors help you pick the best bank/offer as per your eligibility & preferences.

  4. Quick Approval

    • Get instant/within 24-hour approval for eligible candidates.

  5. Loan Disbursement

    • Bank credits the loan amount directly to Aventro Motors for seamless vehicle delivery.

  6. Drive Away

    • Take delivery of your new Aventro Motors car!

Required Documents

  • Identity Proof (Aadhaar Card, Passport, etc.)
  • Address Proof (Utility Bill, Rent Agreement, etc.)
  • Income Proof (Salary Slip/IT Returns)
  • Bank Statements (last 6 months)
  • Passport-size photographs
