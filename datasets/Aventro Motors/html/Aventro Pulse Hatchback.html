Overview

Aventro Pulse Hatchback is the perfect blend of peppy performance, efficiency, and feature-rich technology, making it an ideal city companion. With striking looks and a youthful personality, Pulse is as versatile as your everyday needs.

Key Specifications

  • Body Type: Hatchback
  • Seating Capacity: 5
  • Boot Space: 270L (approx.)
  • Safety Features: Dual airbags, ABS with EBD, Reverse Parking Sensors (on all variants)
  • Infotainment: Upto 11-inch touchscreen (on X & ZX)
  • Advanced Driver Assistance: ADAS from X variant onwards
  • Available in: Petrol, Diesel, Electric, Hybrid
