Model Focus: Aventro Glory SUV

Overview

The Aventro Glory is Aventro’s flagship SUV designed to deliver outstanding comfort, performance, and cutting-edge technology. Blending power, style, and the latest automotive innovations, the Glory caters to all types of drivers—from families to adventure enthusiasts.

Key Specifications

  • Seating Capacity: 5
  • Transmission Options: 6-speed manual / 7-speed automatic (variant specific)
  • Boot Space: 505 litres
  • Infotainment: Up to 11-inch advanced touchscreen system
  • ADAS: Level 1 & 2 (consult variants)
  • Safety: 6 airbags, ABS with EBD, ESC, 360-degree camera (ZX)
  • Tyre Size: 17-inch & 18-inch alloy wheels (variant specific)
  • Upholstery: Premium fabric & leather (ZX)
  • Audio: Standard/Advanced Harman Kardon system (ZX)
  • Notable Features: Sunroof, Automatic Climate Control, Keyless Entry, Wireless Charging
