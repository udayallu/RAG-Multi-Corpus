Introduction

The Aventro Aero is a versatile and dynamic hatchback designed for the urban commuter and small families. With cutting-edge technology, futuristic design, multiple drivetrain options, and rich features across variants, the Aero caters to every need—whether fuel efficiency, ecology, or performance.

Drivetrain Options

  • Petrol: Efficient and peppy for city drives.
  • Diesel: Fuel-saving and torquey for long-distances.
  • Electric: Zero-emission, high torque, and ultra-smooth.
  • Hybrid: Eco-friendly blend of petrol and electric, with great efficiency.

Key Specifications (Standard Features)

  • Body Type: Hatchback
  • Seating: 5
  • Transmission: Manual & Automatic (variant dependent)
  • Safety: Dual airbags, ABS with EBD, Rear Parking Sensors
  • Comfort: Power windows, remote locking, auto climate control
  • Infotainment: Touchscreen (Base depends on variant)

Feature Highlights by Trim

Base

  • 7-inch touchscreen infotainment system
  • Manual AC
  • Standard fabric seats
  • Bluetooth & USB connectivity
  • Dual airbags, ABS, EBD

X Trim (Enhanced Features)

  • 11-inch Infotainment System (Touchscreen)
