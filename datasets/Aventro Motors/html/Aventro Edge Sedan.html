Aventro Edge Sedan

Overview

The Aventro Edge Sedan is a sophisticated blend of contemporary design, innovative technology, and reliable performance. Aimed at customers who value comfort, safety, and modern features, the Edge comes in a variety of engine options, packed with advanced connectivity and safety features for city and highway use alike.

Specifications

  • Class: Sedan
  • Seating Capacity: 5
  • Boot Space: 480 L
  • Transmission: 6-Speed Manual / Automatic (Depending on variant)
  • Airbags: Dual front (Standard), up to 6 in higher variants
  • ABS & EBD: Standard across all variants
  • Wheel Options: 16-inch Alloy Wheels (Standard)
  • Fuel Options: Petrol, Diesel, Electric, Hybrid
  • Dimensions:
    • Length: 4,650 mm
    • Width: 1,780 mm
    • Height: 1,470 mm
    • Wheelbase: 2,700 mm

Features

  • LED DRLs & Headlamps
  • Projector headlamps (from X variant upwards)
  • Cruise Control
  • Wireless Charging (from X variant)
  • Automatic Climate Control (all variants)
  • Rear AC Vent
  • Push Button Start
  • Keyless Entry
  • Rear Parking Sensors (all variants)
  • Electrically Adjustable ORVMs
  • Fast charging (Electric variant)
