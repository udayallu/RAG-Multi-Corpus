Aventro Swift Sedan

Overview

Aventro Swift Sedan is an elegant and performance-driven sedan designed for Indian roads. With a blend of advanced safety, modern features, and fuel-efficient technology, the Swift promises comfort, technology, and style. Available in Petrol, Diesel, Electric, and Hybrid variants.

Key Specifications

  • Body Type: Sedan
  • Seating Capacity: 5
  • Transmission: Manual / Automatic (depending on variant)
  • Infotainment System: Up to 11-inch touchscreen
  • ADAS: Level 1 and Level 2 (on higher trims)
  • Audio: Up to Harman sound system
  • Upholstery: Premium leather seats (on ZX)

Engine & Performance Specs

Notable Features

  • Safety: Dual airbags, ABS with EBD, Rear parking sensors, ESC
  • Technology: 11-inch touchscreen entertainment (X & ZX), Android Auto/Apple CarPlay
  • ADAS: Level 1 (X), Level 2 (ZX)
  • Entertainment: Harman Audio System (ZX)
  • Convenience: Push-button start, Keyless entry
  • Comfort: Automatic climate control, Leather seats (ZX only)
  • Camera: 360º camera (ZX only)
  • Wheels: 16” dual-tone alloy wheels (X & ZX)
  • Lighting: LED headlamps and DRLs
