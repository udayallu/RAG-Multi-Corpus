How to Tow Your Aventro Car

Whether you’ve had a breakdown, a minor accident, or just need to move your Aventro vehicle, towing it safely is critical. Below is a step-by-step guide for both manual and automatic Aventro car models.

Before You Begin

  • Check Owner’s Manual: Refer to your specific model’s owner’s manual for any towing restrictions or recommendations.
  • Warning! Never tow with wheels on the ground if your model is electric. Use a flatbed truck.

Step 1: Gather Necessary Tools

  • Tow strap or tow rope (rated for your car weight)
  • Tow hook (provided with vehicle, usually in trunk/tool kit)
  • Torch (for visibility at night)
  • Warning triangle or cones

Step 2: Locate the Tow Hook Covers

  1. Front and Rear Bumper:
    • Look for small plastic covers on the bumper (often marked by a tow symbol).
  2. Remove the Cover:
    • Use a plastic pry tool or key to open the cover.

Step 3: Attach the Tow Hook

  1. Retrieve the Tow Hook:
    • Found in your car’s tool kit or trunk.
  2. Screw in the Tow Hook:
    • Insert into the exposed thread & rotate clockwise until tight.

Step 4: Connect the Tow Strap or Tow Rope

  • Attach to Tow Hook:
    Ensure the strap/rope is securely fastened to the tow hook.
  • Other Vehicle:
    Attach the other end to the towing vehicle’s tow point.

Step 5: Prepare the Vehicle for Towing

  • Manual Transmission:
    Place in Neutral, release the parking brake.
  • Automatic Transmission:
    Place in Neutral (N), release the parking brake.
    CAUTION: For automatics and hybrids, flatbed towing is recommended to avoid transmission damage.
  • Electric Vehicles:
    ONLY FLATBED TOWING. Do not tow with wheels on ground.

Step 6: Safety Measures

  • Place warning triangle/cones at a safe distance behind both vehicles.
  • Switch ON hazard lights on both vehicles.
  • Ensure both drivers agree on signaling and communication.

Step 7: Towing

  • The towing vehicle should accelerate slowly – no sudden jerks.
  • Maximum speed should not exceed 25 km/h.
  • Keep the towed vehicle’s ignition ON (to prevent steering lock and allow brake assist).
  • Steer and brake gently in the towed vehicle.

Step 8: After Towing

  • Park both vehicles safely.
  • Unscrew the tow hook and store it back with your tools.
  • Replace the plastic tow hook cover.

Important Safety Notice

  • Never tow on highways unless necessary.
  • Do not allow passengers in the towed vehicle.
  • If in doubt, use Aventro Roadside Assistance or professional towing.
