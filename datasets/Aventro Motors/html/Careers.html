Current Job Openings

1. Sales Executive

Location: Aventro Tower, Sector 18, Gurgaon, Haryana
Department: Sales & Marketing
Job Type: Full-time

Key Responsibilities: - Generate and manage leads for new car sales - Explain features and advantages of Aventro models (Petrol, Diesel, Electric, Hybrid) - Build and maintain customer relationships - Achieve monthly and quarterly sales targets

Qualifications: - Graduate (any discipline) - 1-2 years automotive sales experience preferred - Excellent communication and presentation skills

2. Service Technician (Electric Vehicles)

Location: Okhla Industrial Area, New Delhi / Electronic City, Bengaluru
Department: After Sales Service
Job Type: Full-time

Key Responsibilities: - Diagnose and repair electric vehicle systems (battery, motor, electronics) - Perform regular maintenance and system checks - Coordinate with technical teams for problem resolution

Qualifications: - ITI/Diploma in Automobile/Electrical/Mechanical Engineering - Experience in servicing electric/hybrid vehicles preferred - Strong technical troubleshooting skills

3. Quality Assurance Engineer

Location: Aventro Tower, Sector 18, Gurgaon, Haryana
Department: Production & R&D
Job Type: Full-time

Key Responsibilities: - Conduct quality checks on car assemblies and component parts - Liaise with suppliers for part quality (e.g., Engine, Battery Pack, Brake Pads) - Prepare QA reports and suggest corrective actions

Qualifications: - B.E/B.Tech in Automobile/Mechanical Engineering - Knowledge of quality systems/standards in automotive industry

4. Customer Support Associate

Location: Gurugram, Haryana / Work from Home
Department: Customer Care
Job Type: Full-time/Contract

Key Responsibilities: - Handle customer inquiries about car models, servicing, and locations - Provide assistance for insurance claims, roadside support, and maintenance booking

Qualifications: - Graduate in any stream - Experience in customer service roles preferred - Good command of English and Hindi

5. Parts Manager

Location: Andheri East, Mumbai / Vastrapur, Ahmedabad
Department: Spare Parts Management
Job Type: Full-time

Key Responsibilities: - Oversee inventory of automotive parts (Engine Assembly to Custom Accessories) - Manage supplier relationships and logistics - Coordinate with Service Centers for part requests and fulfillment

Qualifications: - Graduate/Diploma in related field - 2+ years’ experience in automotive parts or inventory management
