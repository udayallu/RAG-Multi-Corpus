Genuine Spare Parts – Details & Price (Industry Standard Estimates)

1. Engine Assembly

  • Description: The core mechanical component that converts fuel into mechanical power for propulsion.
  • Applicable Models: All Petrol, Diesel, and Hybrid variants
  • Industry Standard Price: ₹1,20,000 – ₹2,50,000 (varies by engine capacity and technology)
  • Warranty: 2 years or 50,000 km (whichever earlier)
  • Note: For Electric variants, Engine Assembly is replaced by Battery Pack/Motor assembly.

2. Battery Pack

  • Description: High-capacity rechargeable battery powering Electric and Hybrid models.
  • Applicable Models: Electric & Hybrid variants
  • Industry Standard Price:
    • Electric: ₹2,00,000 – ₹4,50,000 (depending on kWh rating)
    • Hybrid: ₹60,000 – ₹1,50,000
  • Warranty: 8 years or 1,60,000 km (whichever earlier)
  • Note: Prices depend on size (kWh), chemistry, and import duties.

3. Transmission

  • Description: Transmits engine/motor power to wheels, manages speed and torque.
  • Applicable Models: All variants (specific tech for Automatic, Manual, or e-Drive)
  • Industry Standard Price:
    • Manual: ₹65,000 – ₹1,20,000
    • Automatic (AT/CVT/DCT): ₹1,10,000 – ₹2,00,000
  • Warranty: 2 years or 40,000 km (whichever earlier)

4. Brake Pads

  • Description: Key component for vehicle stopping, includes front and rear sets.
  • Applicable Models: All, with some EVs using regenerative braking for longevity
  • Industry Standard Price:
    • Front Set: ₹3,000 – ₹6,000
    • Rear Set: ₹2,500 – ₹5,000
  • Warranty: 1 year or 20,000 km

5. Suspension System

  • Description: System of springs, dampers, and linkages for ride comfort and handling.
  • Applicable Models: All models and variants
  • Industry Standard Price: ₹18,000 – ₹45,000 (per axle, including shock absorbers & bushings)
  • Warranty: 1 year or 25,000 km

Notes

  • Prices are indicative, ex-showroom, exclude labor, taxes, and may vary by region/model.
