# Aventro Spark Hatchback

Welcome to the official specifications page for the **Aventro Spark**, a stylish, compact, and efficient hatchback brought to you by Aventro Motors Pvt. Ltd.

---

## Overview

The **Spark Hatchback** is designed for city comfort, agility, and fuel efficiency, all while being packed with modern features. Whether you opt for the practical Base, feature-rich X, or the technologically advanced ZX variant, you get reliability—a true value for your family.

## Features (Highlights)

- LED Headlamps and Tail Lamps (ZX only)
- Premium Fabric or Leather Seats (ZX)
- Push Button Start (X & ZX)
- Rear Parking Sensors (All trims)
- Cruise Control (X & ZX)
- Automatic Climate Control (X & ZX)
- 11-inch Infotainment Touchscreen (X & ZX)
- Harman Audio System (ZX only)

## Special Variant Features

- **X Variant**:
  - 11-inch touchscreen infotainment system
  - ADAS Level 1 driver assists (auto emergency braking, rear collision warning)
  - Automatic Climate control
  - Alloy Wheels

- **ZX Variant**:
  - 11-inch touchscreen with Harman sound system
  - 360° Surround View Camera
  - ADAS Level 2 (includes lane keep assist, adaptive cruise control)
  - Leather seats
  - Premium LED lighting
