# Aventro Motors Pvt. Ltd.

## Model Overview: Zoom SUV

### About Zoom SUV

The Aventro Zoom is a versatile and robust SUV, perfect for families and adventure-lovers alike. It offers a dynamic blend of performance, comfort, and next-generation technology, making it a top contender in its segment. Zoom is available in four powertrains—Petrol, Diesel, Electric, and Hybrid—with three trim levels each: Base, X, and ZX.

## Powertrain Options

- **Petrol Engine:** Responsive and efficient for city and highway drives.
- **Diesel Engine:** High-torque performer for long-distance cruising.
- **Electric Variant:** Zero emission, fast charging, and long range for the sustainable commuter.
- **Hybrid Variant:** Intelligent fuel efficiency blending petrol and electric power.

---

## Key Features & Technology

- **ADAS (Advanced Driver Assistance Systems):**  
  - Level 1 (X): Lane departure warning, forward collision warning  
  - Level 2 (ZX): Lane keep assist, adaptive cruise control, emergency braking
- **Infotainment:** 7-inch (Base), 11-inch (X & ZX, Harman system on ZX)
- **360° Camera (ZX only):** For effortless parking and tight situations
- **Connected Car Suite:** App-based remote commands (lock/unlock, location, diagnostics)
- **Safety:** Up to 6 airbags, ESC, ISOfix child seat mounts
- **Luxury:** Panoramic sunroof (ZX), leather seats (ZX)
- **Comfort:** Rear AC vents, large boot, spacious cabin
