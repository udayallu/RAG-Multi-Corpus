# Aventro Motors Pvt. Ltd.

## Bolt Sedan

---

**Aventro Bolt Sedan** is a premium mid-size sedan from Aventro Motors, designed for comfort, performance, and innovation. It is available in four fuel types—Petrol, Diesel, Electric, and Hybrid—each offered in three trims: Base, X, and ZX.

---

## Key Specifications

- **Body Type:** Sedan
- **Seating Capacity:** 5
- **Boot Space:** 470 litres (approx.)
- **Transmission:** Manual / Automatic (varies by variant)
- **Fuel Options:** Petrol, Diesel, Electric, Hybrid
- **Infotainment System:** Up to 11-inch touchscreen (X & ZX)
- **ADAS:** Level 1 (X), Level 2 (ZX)
- **Sunroof:** Available in ZX trim
- **Upholstery:** Fabric (Base & X), Leather (ZX)
- **Speakers:** Premium Harmon System (ZX)
- **Safety:** ABS, EBD, Multiple Airbags (Standard across all variants)

## Highlighted Features by Trim

### **Base**
- Touchscreen infotainment
- Climate control
- Reverse sensors
- ABS, EBD, dual airbags
- Fabric seats

### **X** (adds over Base)
- 11-inch advanced infotainment system
- ADAS Level 1 (Forward Collision Warning, Lane Departure Warning, Adaptive Cruise)
- Enhanced interior accents

### **ZX** (adds over X)
- 360° surround view camera
- 11-inch infotainment system with Harmon premium audio
- ADAS Level 2 (Auto Emergency Braking, Enhanced Lane Keep Assist, Adaptive Cruise)
- Powered leather seats
- Powered sunroof
