# Aventro Motors Guide: Connecting Smartwatch Connectivity, Works with Alexa, and CarPlay

Welcome to your Aventro vehicle’s connected features guide! This document explains how to connect and use:
- **Smartwatch Connectivity**
- **Works with Alexa**
- **Apple CarPlay**

Below, you’ll find step-by-step setup instructions and troubleshooting tips for each feature.

---

## 1. Smartwatch Connectivity

### Overview

Smartwatch connectivity allows you to interact with select vehicle functions directly from your smartwatch. This may include lock/unlock, remote start, finding your car, and vehicle health reports.

### Compatible Smartwatches
- Apple Watch (watchOS 6 and above)
- Android Wear OS Watches (Wear OS 2 and above)
- Samsung Galaxy Watches (Tizen 4 and above)

### Steps to Connect

#### **A. Install the Aventro Connect App**
1. On your smartphone, download the **Aventro Connect** app from Google Play Store or Apple App Store.
2. Create or log into your Aventro account.

#### **B. Pair Your Smartwatch**
1. Ensure your smartwatch is paired with your smartphone via Bluetooth and both are connected to the Internet.
2. On your watch, open the relevant app store (Apple Watch App Store, Google Play for Wear OS, or Galaxy Store).
3. Search for **Aventro Connect** and install it.
4. Open the app on your smartwatch. You may be prompted to sign in with your Aventro account or grant permissions.
5. On your smartphone’s Aventro Connect app, go to _Settings_ > _Device Connections_ > _Add Smartwatch_.
6. Follow on-screen instructions to authorize and link the smartwatch.

#### **C. Using Smartwatch Features**
- **Lock/Unlock:** Tap the respective button in the app.
- **Remote Start:** Press and hold the remote start icon in the app.
- **Find My Car:** View your vehicle’s last parked location.

#### **Troubleshooting**
- Make sure Bluetooth is enabled on both watch and phone.
- Both devices must be within range.
- Both devices must have an internet connection.

## 2. Works with Alexa

### Overview

Control your Aventro car using Alexa-enabled devices and voice commands: lock/unlock car, remote start, check fuel/battery status, etc.

### Prerequisites
- Alexa-enabled device (Echo Dot, Echo Show, Amazon App)
- Aventro Connect account
- Vehicle registered on Aventro Connect app

### Steps to Connect

1. On your smartphone, open the **Amazon Alexa** app.
2. Tap the menu and select **Skills & Games**.
3. Search for **Aventro Motors** (Aventro Connect Skill).
4. Tap **Enable**.
5. Log in with your Aventro Connect account to link it.
6. Grant requested permissions (vehicle control, location, etc.).

#### Example Voice Commands
- "Alexa, ask Aventro to start my car."
- "Alexa, ask Aventro if my car is locked."
- "Alexa, ask Aventro how much battery my Aero has left."

#### Troubleshooting
- Ensure car has active Internet connectivity (SIM/eSIM).
- User permissions in Aventro Connect app must be up to date.
- If unresponsive, try disabling and re-enabling the Aventro skill in the Alexa app.

## 3. Apple CarPlay

### Overview

Apple CarPlay seamlessly integrates your iPhone with your Aventro vehicle’s infotainment system. Use Maps, Music, Calls, and Messages hands-free.

### Prerequisites
- iPhone 6 or newer (iOS 13 or above)
- Genuine Apple Lightning cable **or** wireless pairing (in supported models)

### Steps to Connect

#### **A. Wired CarPlay Connection**
1. Start your car and turn on the infotainment system.
2. Plug your iPhone into the car’s USB port with a genuine Apple Lightning cable.
3. CarPlay prompt will appear on your infotainment display. Tap **Allow** on your iPhone if asked.

#### **B. Wireless CarPlay (If Supported)**
1. Enable both Bluetooth and Wi-Fi on your iPhone.
2. On the car’s infotainment display, go to _Settings_ > _Apple CarPlay_ > _Pair New Device_.
3. On your iPhone, go to _Settings_ > _General_ > _CarPlay_ and select your Aventro car.
4. Follow on-screen instructions to pair wirelessly.

### Common CarPlay Features
- Voice: Press and hold the steering wheel’s voice button for Siri.
- Maps: Use Apple Maps, Google Maps, Waze, etc.
- Messages: Listen and respond hands-free.
- Music & Audio: Apple Music, Spotify, Podcasts, etc.

#### Troubleshooting
- Restart your iPhone and the infotainment system if CarPlay won’t connect.
- Use only Apple-certified cables for wired connections.
- Make sure your Aventro model supports wireless CarPlay if attempting wireless.
