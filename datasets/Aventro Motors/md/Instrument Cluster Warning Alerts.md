# Instrument Cluster Warning Alerts

The instrument cluster in Aventro Motors vehicles is designed to inform the driver about the status of various vehicle systems. Each symbol or indicator light provides information on system performance, potential malfunctions, or urgent warnings requiring immediate action.

Below is a list of common warning alerts you may see in Aventro Motors cars, along with their meanings:

---

## 1. **Engine Warning Light (Check Engine)**
- **Meaning:** Indicates a problem with the engine or emissions control system. Immediate diagnosis is recommended.

## 2. **Battery Warning Light**
- **Meaning:** The battery is not charging properly. This could signal alternator failure, battery issues, or wiring problems.

## 3. **Oil Pressure Warning Light**
- **Meaning:** Low oil pressure detected. Stop the engine and check oil levels to avoid engine damage.

## 4. **Brake System Warning Light**
- **Meaning:** Possible issues with the brake system such as low fluid, worn brake pads, or handbrake engaged.

## 5. **ABS Warning Light**
- **Meaning:** Indicates a malfunction in the Anti-lock Braking System. Normal brakes may function, but ABS will not.

## 6. **Airbag Warning Light**
- **Meaning:** There may be a problem with the airbag system; airbags may not deploy in a collision.

## 7. **Coolant Temperature Warning Light**
- **Meaning:** Engine is overheating. Stop the vehicle in a safe place and check coolant levels.

## 8. **Transmission Warning Light**
- **Meaning:** Transmission malfunction, possible overheating or system fault. Immediate attention required.

## 9. **Tire Pressure Monitoring System (TPMS) Light**
- **Meaning:** One or more tires have low pressure or thereâ€™s a sensor malfunction.

## 10. **Traction Control Warning Light**
- **Meaning:** Indicates that the traction control system is currently in use or there is a system fault.

## 11. **ESP/BAS Warning Light**
- **Symbol:** ESP/BAS
- **Meaning:** Problem with Electronic Stability Program/Brake Assist System.

## 12. **Power Steering Warning Light**
- **Meaning:** Malfunction in the power steering system; steering may feel heavy.

## 13. **Fuel Level Warning Light**
- **Meaning:** Low fuel level; refuel soon.

## 14. **Glow Plug Warning Light (Diesel)**
- **Meaning:** Indicates pre-heating of diesel engine or a fault in the glow plug system.

## 15. **Hybrid/Electric System Warning Light**
- **Symbol:** Hybrid/EV Symbol
- **Meaning:** Malfunction in the hybrid or electric powertrain. Service required.

## 16. **Service Reminder Light**
- **Symbol:** Spanner/Wrench symbol
- **Meaning:** Indicates that regular maintenance is due.

## 17. **Seatbelt Reminder Light**
- **Meaning:** Driver or passenger (depending on sensor) seatbelt is not fastened.

## 18. **Door Ajar Warning Light**
- **Symbol:** Car with Doors Open
- **Meaning:** One or more doors are not fully closed.

## 19. **Washer Fluid Warning Light**
- **Meaning:** Windshield washer fluid is low.

## 20. **High Beam Indicator**
- **Meaning:** High beam headlights are turned on. (This is informational, not a warning.)

---

## Important Notes
- If any **red** warning light appears, stop the vehicle in a safe location as soon as possible and consult your Aventro Motors dealer or the owner's manual.
- **Yellow/amber** lights indicate caution; check the system soon.
- For **hybrid/electric models**, specific alerts will provide system details regarding battery, charging, and electric drive functions.
