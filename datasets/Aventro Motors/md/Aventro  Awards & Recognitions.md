# Aventro Motors Pvt. Ltd. - Awards & Recognitions

## Overview

Aventro Motors Pvt. Ltd. has been recognized for its excellence in research, innovation, customer satisfaction, and commitment to sustainability within the automotive industry. The company’s state-of-the-art vehicles and services have earned several prestigious awards and industry recognitions.

---

## Awards & Achievements

### 2024

- **“Car Manufacturer of the Year”**  
  *AutoTech India Awards 2024*  
  For consistent innovation and leadership in India’s passenger vehicle market.

- **“Best Electric Vehicle Launch”**  
  *Green Wheels Expo 2024*  
  Awarded for the successful launch of the Aventro Aero Electric variant.

- **“Best Customer Service Experience”**  
  *National Auto Customer Choice Awards 2024*  
  For outstanding after-sales and service center network across India.
