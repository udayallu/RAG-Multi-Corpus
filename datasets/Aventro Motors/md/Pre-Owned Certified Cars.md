## **Aventro 380+ Checkpoints: The Gold Standard in Assurance**

All Aventro Certified Pre-Owned Cars go through a rigorous inspection and reconditioning process at our state-of-the-art service centers. Our checklist covers every detail, including:

<details>
<summary><strong>1. Engine & Transmission (50+ Checks)</strong></summary>

- Complete engine assessment (mounts, leaks, coils)
- Compression and performance check
- Oil filter and air filter health
- Starter motor, alternator, radiator, coolant system
- Transmission system & clutch operation
- Fuel injector & fuel pump condition
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>2. Battery & Electrical Systems (40+ Checks)</strong></summary>

- Battery health & terminals
- Starter/ alternator output
- Dashboard lights and alerts
- Headlamps, tail lamps, fog lamps, indicators
- Horn, internal cabin lights, switches, wiring
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>3. Suspension & Brakes (30+ Checks)</strong></summary>

- Shock absorbers, mounts, bushings
- Brake pads/ shoes, discs/ drums, fluid
- Brake response & pedal feedback
- Handbrake/ parking brake operation
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>4. Exterior & Interior Body (70+ Checks)</strong></summary>

- Paint and body surface integrity
- Bumper, bonnet, roof, door panels
- Grade of scratch/ dent (if any)
- Side mirrors, door locks, wiper operation
- Alloy wheels, tyre tread, spare tyre, jack
- Windshield, window glasses, water ingress
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>5. HVAC & Comfort Systems (25+ Checks)</strong></summary>

- AC compressor, cooling, heater function
- Air vents, blower speed, climate sensor
- Seat adjustment/ heating
- Cabin filter and dust checks
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>6. Infotainment & Connectivity (20+ Checks)</strong></summary>

- Dashboard display, infotainment system
- Audio/ video, Bluetooth, AUX/ USB
- Navigation, speakers, mic check
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>7. Safety & Control Systems (40+ Checks)</strong></summary>

- Airbags, seatbelts, pre-tensioners
- ABS, EBD modules, sensors
- Alarm system, immobilizer, hazard signals
- Steering column, wheel alignment 
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>8. Road Test Parameters (15+ Checks)</strong></summary>

- Engine response, acceleration, noise & vibration
- Gear shift, steering feedback, road stability
- Suspension and brake performance at speed
- Unusual noises, warning signs
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>...And More:</strong></summary>

- Chassis, underbody inspection, fluid leaks
- OBD diagnostics, emissions compliance
- Custom accessory condition
</details>
