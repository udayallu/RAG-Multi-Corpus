## Quick Overview

The **Aventro Grand SUV** is the flagship, full-size SUV offering from Aventro Motors, combining power, technology, and sophistication. Available in Petrol, Diesel, Electric, and Hybrid, the Grand SUV is designed for families and enthusiasts who seek space, comfort, safety, and the latest automotive features.

## Core Specifications

- **Dimensions**:  
  - Length: ~4850mm  
  - Width: ~1950mm  
  - Height: ~1810mm  
  - Wheelbase: ~2850mm  
- **Seating**: 7-seater  
- **Boot Space**: Up to 700 Litres  
- **Powertrain Options**:  
  - Petrol: 2.0L Turbocharged, 6-Speed AT  
  - Diesel: 2.2L Turbo Diesel, 8-Speed AT  
  - Electric: 85 kWh Battery (Range: approx. 480 km*)  
  - Hybrid: Turbo Petrol + Electric Motor (Combined output TBA)

## Notable Features (Trim-wise)

### Base Trim (All Fuel Types)

- Projector Headlamps
- Dual Zone Automatic Climate Control
- Rear Parking Sensors and Camera
- 8-inch Touchscreen Display
- Cruise Control
- Standard Safety & Airbags (6 airbags)

### X Trim (All Fuel Types)

- **11-inch Advanced Infotainment System**
- **ADAS Level 1 (Basic driver assist features: Lane Departure, Collision Warning)**
- Wireless Android Auto & Apple CarPlay
- Front Parking Sensors

### ZX Trim (All Fuel Types)

- **360-Degree Camera**
- **11-inch Touchscreen with Harman Audio System**
- **ADAS Level 2 (Enhanced: Adaptive Cruise, Traffic Jam Assist)**
- **Premium Leather Seats**
- Panoramic Sunroof
- Powered Tailgate
- Ventilated Front Seats
