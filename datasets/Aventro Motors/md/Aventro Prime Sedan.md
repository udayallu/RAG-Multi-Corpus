## Overview

The **Aventro Prime** is an advanced sedan that blends style, comfort, and technology for the discerning modern driver. Designed for those who seek luxury and performance, the Prime boasts a sleek aerodynamic profile, a spacious interior, and state-of-the-art features across its variants. The Prime is available in four efficient powertrains: Petrol, Diesel, Electric, and Hybrid.

---

## Key Specifications

- **Model Type:** Sedan
- **Seating Capacity:** 5
- **Transmission:** 6-speed Manual / 6-speed Automatic (Varies by trim)
- **Safety:** 6 Airbags, ABS with EBD, ESP, Hill Hold Assist
- **Length x Width x Height:** 4675 x 1815 x 1445 mm
- **Boot Space:** 450 L
