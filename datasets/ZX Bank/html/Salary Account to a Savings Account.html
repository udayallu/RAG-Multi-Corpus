How to Convert Your Salary Account to a Savings Account at ZX Bank

If your employment has changed, or your salary account is no longer receiving regular salary credits, you may need to convert your Salary Account to a regular Savings Account. At ZX Bank, this process is straightforward. Here’s how you can do it:

Why Convert Your Salary Account?

  • Salary accounts are typically provided to employees for salary credits and have special benefits.
  • If salary credits stop (for 3+ months), your account may be automatically converted to a Savings Account by the bank, but manual conversion avoids service interruptions.
  • Savings accounts have different features and requirements, including minimum balance criteria.

Steps to Convert Salary Account to Savings Account

1. Visit Your Home Branch

  • Locate your nearest ZX Bank branch (especially the one where you opened your salary account).
  • Carry valid proof of identity (Aadhaar, PAN Card, Passport, etc.) and account passbook/debit card.

2. Fill Out the Account Conversion Request Form

  • Request a Salary to Savings Account Conversion Form at the branch.
  • Fill in your details, mention your account number, and select “Convert to Savings Account.”
  • Attach self-attested copies of requested KYC documents (if required).

3. Submit the Form and Documents

  • Hand over the filled form and documents to the branch staff.
  • The staff will verify your documents and initiate the conversion process.

4. Maintain Minimum Balance

  • Ensure you maintain the required minimum balance for Savings Accounts as per ZX Bank guidelines, to avoid charges.

5. Get Confirmation

  • You will receive confirmation via SMS/email once the conversion is complete.
  • Existing debit card, chequebook, and account number typically remain unchanged.

Alternative: Conversion via Digital Banking

ZX Bank also enables digital requests:

  1. Open the ZX Bank Asia Mobile App or log into NetBanking.
  2. Go to “Service Requests” > “Convert Salary Account to Savings Account.”
  3. Fill in details and upload necessary KYC docs, if asked.
  4. Receive confirmation after processing.
