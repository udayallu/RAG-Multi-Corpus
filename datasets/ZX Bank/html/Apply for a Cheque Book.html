How to Apply for a Cheque Book at ZX Bank

Applying for a cheque book from ZX Bank is simple and can be done through multiple convenient channels. Follow the steps below to request your new cheque book.

1. Via ZX Bank Mobile App

  1. Login: Open the ZX Bank Asia app and log in using your credentials or biometric authentication.
  2. Navigate: Go to Services or Request Services on the home menu.
  3. Select: Tap on Cheque Book Request.
  4. Choose Account: Select the account for which you need the cheque book.
  5. Confirm Details: Enter delivery address (if different from registered address).
  6. Submit: Review and submit your request.
    You’ll receive a confirmation SMS/email with the estimated delivery time.

2. Through NetBanking

  1. Log in to ZX Bank NetBanking.
  2. Go to Services > Cheque Book Request.
  3. Select your account and number of leaves required.
  4. Verify your address and submit the request.
  5. Track status online under Request History.

3. At Branch

  1. Visit your nearest ZX Bank branch.
  2. Fill out the Cheque Book Request Form at the counter.
  3. Submit it to the bank officer along with valid ID, if required.
  4. Collect acknowledgment receipt with expected dispatch date.

4. Via ZX Bank ATM

  1. Insert your ZX Bank debit card and enter your PIN.
  2. Go to Services > Cheque Book Request.
  3. Select account and number of leaves.
  4. Confirm the request to complete the process.

5. ZX Bank Customer Care

  • Call ZX Bank’s 24x7 helpline: 1800-XXX-XXXX (toll-free in India)
  • Verify your details with the customer care executive.
  • Place your cheque book request and note down the reference number.

ℹ️ Frequently Asked Questions

  • Delivery Time: Cheque books are dispatched within 3–7 working days.
  • Charges: The first cheque book of the year is usually free. Subsequent requests may incur standard charges.
  • Track Status: Use the mobile app or NetBanking to check dispatch and delivery status.
  • Change Address: Update your address before placing the request to ensure correct delivery.

For more assistance, visit www.zxbank.asia or contact your nearest ZX Bank branch.
