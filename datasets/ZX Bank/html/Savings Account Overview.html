ZX Bank Ltd. – Savings Account Overview

ZX Bank’s Savings Account is designed to give you a blend of convenience, security, and rewarding benefits, with a focus on easy access and digital-first banking. Below you’ll find detailed information on features, application process, minimum balance, and the exclusive Savings Account Kit offered to new account holders.

Savings Account Features

  • Attractive Interest Rates: Earn competitive interest on your daily balance.
  • Zero or Low Minimum Balance Options: Choose accounts that suit your needs and avoid penalties.
  • Instant Account Activation: Through fully digital onboarding (e-KYC) and AI-powered support.
  • Free International Debit Card: Accepted worldwide for shopping, travel, and online payments.
  • Seamless Digital Access: Manage your account via ZX Bank Asia mobile app and NetBanking.
  • UPI & QR Payments: Fast money transfers and secure payments on the go.
  • Reward Points: Earn rewards on all debit transactions.
  • Safe & Secure: Biometric login, card controls, and transaction alerts for peace of mind.
  • Nomination Facility: Assign a nominee for account safety.

How to Apply for a ZX Bank Savings Account

You can open a ZX Bank Savings Account easily through these channels:

1. Via Mobile App or Website

  • Download the ZX Bank Asia app (Android/iOS) or visit www.zxbank.asia
  • Click “Open Savings Account”
  • Fill in your details and upload necessary documents (PAN/ID, Address Proof)
  • Complete e-KYC via video or Aadhaar OTP
  • Account activation is done within minutes!

2. At Branch

  • Visit your nearest ZX Bank branch
  • Submit KYC documents with a duly filled application form
  • Staff will assist you with on-the-spot e-KYC
  • Collect your Welcome Kit instantly
