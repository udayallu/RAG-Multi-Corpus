How to Apply for a Current Account

Online Process: 1. Visit www.zxbank.asia. 2. Click on ‘Open Current Account’. 3. Complete digital e-KYC with Aadhaar and PAN. 4. Upload business documents (as applicable). 5. Choose branch & verify mobile/email via OTP. 6. Receive instant account number and digital onboarding kit.

Offline Process: 1. Visit nearest ZX Bank branch. 2. Fill the current account opening form with your business KYC. 3. Submit documents (ID/Address proof, business registration). 4. Complete in-person biometric verification. 5. Account activates within 24 hours.
