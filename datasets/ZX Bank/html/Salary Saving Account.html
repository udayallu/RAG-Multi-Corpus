  • Zero Balance Facility: No need to maintain a minimum balance — enjoy complete convenience.

  • Free Debit Card: Complimentary international debit card for secure and easy transactions.
  • Auto Sweep Fixed Deposit: Automatic transfer of surplus funds into Fixed Deposits for higher returns.

  • Preferential Loan Rates: Special interest rates on personal loans, home loans, and car loans.
  • Bill Payments & Recharge: Pay utility bills and recharge mobile/DTH instantly via the ZX Bank app.
  • AI-powered Banking Assistant: 24x7 help from Zia, your smart banking assistant.

How to Apply for a Salary Saving Account

Online Steps

  1. Visit www.zxbank.asia or Download the ZX Bank Asia Mobile App.
  2. Click on “Open Salary Saving Account”.
  3. Complete e-KYC verification: Upload Aadhaar, PAN, and latest salary slip.
  4. Fill out the application form: Provide basic and employment details.
  5. Review and submit: Verify details and submit application. Receive account number instantly.

Branch Process

  1. Visit your nearest ZX Bank branch.
  2. Ask for the Salary Saving Account Opening Form.
  3. Submit documents: ID proof, address proof, PAN card, and salary slip.
  4. Complete biometric/KYC process with bank representative.
  5. Receive account kit and activation confirmation.
