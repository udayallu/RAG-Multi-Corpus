ZX Bank – NetBanking Guide

What is NetBanking?

ZX Bank NetBanking is a secure online banking platform for individuals and businesses to manage accounts, perform transactions, pay bills, and access banking services anytime, anywhere.

Features

  • Account balance & statement viewing
  • Fund transfers (within ZX Bank & other banks)
  • Bill payments & mobile/DTH recharge
  • Open/close Fixed Deposits & Recurring Deposits
  • Loan & Credit Card management
  • Utility features: Stop cheque, request cheque book, manage beneficiaries

How to Activate ZX Bank NetBanking

1. Self-Registration (Online)

  1. Visit ZX Bank Asia Website
  2. Click on ‘NetBanking Login’ and select ‘New User/Register’
  3. Enter:
    • Customer ID (from passbook/account statement)
    • Registered mobile number
    • Verification via OTP
  4. Set your NetBanking username and password.
  5. Login and set transactional password & security questions.

2. Activation via Branch

  • Visit any ZX Bank branch.
  • Fill NetBanking registration form.
  • Receive welcome kit/activation link on mobile/email.
  • Set your login and transaction password.

3. Activation via Mobile App

  • Download the ZX Bank Asia app (Android/iOS).
  • Select ‘Register for NetBanking’.
  • Follow in-app instructions (similar to online self-registration).

Security Tips

  • Never share your NetBanking password/OTP.
  • Update password regularly.
  • Login only via official website or ZX Bank Asia app.
  • Enable two-factor authentication.
