## ## How to Apply for a Bike Loan

### 1. **Online via ZX Bank Asia App or Website**
   - **Step 1:** Download the **ZX Bank Asia** App (Android/iOS) or visit [www.zxbank.asia](https://www.zxbank.asia)
   - **Step 2:** Select “Apply for Bike Loan” under the Loans section
   - **Step 3:** Fill in your details and upload required documents (e-KYC, income proof, quotation of bike)
   - **Step 4:** Get instant approval and track your status via app

### 2. **At Your Nearest ZX Bank Branch**
   - Visit any ZX Bank branch with basic KYC documents and bike quotation
   - Complete the application form with assistance from our staff
   - Get approval and documentation assistance

## ## Eligibility

- **Age:** 21–60 years
- **Salaried/Self-employed** individuals & business owners
- **Minimum Income:** ₹8,000/month (varies by region)
- **CIBIL Score:** Above 650 preferred

## ## Required Documents

- Identity Proof (Aadhaar/Passport/PAN)

- Recent Photograph
- Income Proof (Salary slip/ITR/bank statement)
