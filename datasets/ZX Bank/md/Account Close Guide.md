# ZX Bank – Account Closure Guide

Closing your account with ZX Bank is simple and secure. Below you will find instructions for closing **Savings Accounts**, **Current Accounts**, and **Salary Accounts**.

---

## 1. How to Close a Savings Account

**Steps:**
1. **Visit Your Home Branch**  
   Bring valid photo ID (e.g., Aadhaar, Passport) and your passbook/debit card.

2. **Fill Out the Account Closure Form**  
   Request and complete the ‘Savings Account Closure Form’ at the branch.

3. **Settle Dues**  
   Ensure all cheque books and debit cards are returned. Settle any outstanding charges.

4. **Withdraw Remaining Balance**  
   The final balance will be paid in cash or transferred to another account of your choice.

5. **Get Confirmation**  
   Obtain an account closure acknowledgment receipt.

**Can I apply online?**  
Currently, ZX Bank requires in-branch verification for savings account closures.

---
## 2. How to Close a Current Account

**Steps:**
1. **Visit the Opening Branch**  
   Bring company/entity proofs (for business accounts) and authorized signatory’s ID.

2. **Submit Account Closure Form**  
   Fill and sign the ‘Current Account Closure Form’. For joint or partnership accounts, all account holders/signatories must sign.

3. **Return Documents and Cheques**  
   Return all unused cheque leaves, debit/ATM cards & passbook.

4. **Settle Pending Dues**  
   Clear any outstanding overdrafts, fees, or charges.

5. **Receive Closure Confirmation**  
   Ask for a written confirmation/receipt from the bank.

**Note:** Funds will be transferred as per your instructions, or withdrawn in cash.

---
## 3. How to Close a Salary Account

**Steps:**
1. **Inform Your Employer**  
   Notify your HR/payroll department, especially if it’s a corporate-linked salary account.

2. **Visit Any ZX Bank Branch**  
   Carry your photo ID and debit card.

3. **Submit Account Closure Form**  
   Fill out the ‘Salary Account Closure Form’. Return cheque book and debit card.

4. **Withdraw the Remaining Balance**  
   You may choose to transfer or withdraw the amount.

5. **Obtain Acknowledgment**  
   Take a receipt for closure from the bank.

**Must-Know:**  
If your salary is not credited for 3 consecutive months, the salary account may convert to a savings account, with related charges applicable.
