# ZX Bank Bangladesh Branch Network

ZX Bank maintains a robust presence in Bangladesh, facilitating financial inclusion and growth through a network of strategically located branches. With a total of 18 branches distributed across major regions—Dhaka, Chittagong, Khulna, Rajshahi, and Sylhet—the bank ensures accessible banking services for individuals and businesses alike. Each branch offers comprehensive banking facilities, from retail and corporate banking to digital onboarding and remittance support.
