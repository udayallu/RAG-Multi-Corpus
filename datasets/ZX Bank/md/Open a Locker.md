# How to Open a Locker at ZX Bank Branch

Opening a locker (safe deposit box) at a ZX Bank branch is a secure way to safeguard your valuables and important documents. Follow these simple steps to apply for and access a locker at any of our branches across India, Sri Lanka, Bhutan, or Nepal.

---

## Step 1: Check Locker Availability

- Visit your nearest **ZX Bank branch** or call the branch directly.
- Inquire about locker sizes and availability.
- Popular sizes: Small, Medium, Large (availability may vary by location).

---

## Step 2: Eligibility Criteria

- **Existing ZX Bank customer:** You must have (or open) a savings or current account.
- **KYC Compliance:** Ensure your KYC (Know Your Customer) details are updated.
    - Valid photo ID (Aadhaar/Passport/Driving License)
    - Address proof
    - Recent passport-sized photo

---

## Step 3: Locker Application Process

1. **Visit the Branch:** Bring original documents and photocopies.
2. **Fill Application Form:** Request and complete the ‘Locker Facility Application Form’ at the branch.
3. **Submit KYC Documents:** Attach required KYC documents to your application.
4. **Locker Agreement:** Read and sign the Locker Hirer Agreement provided by the bank.

---

## Step 4: Security Deposit & Rent Payment

- Pay the **refundable security deposit** (locker rent may vary by size and city).
- Locker rent is typically paid **annually**.
- Payment can be made via cash, cheque, or direct bank transfer.

---

## Step 5: Locker Allotment & Access

- Upon approval, a locker number will be allotted.
- Receive the **locker keys** (usually two keys; bank does not keep duplicates).
- The bank officer will guide you on locker location and operating procedure.

---

## Step 6: Operating the Locker

- Locker can be operated **during branch working hours**.
- Visit the branch with your locker keys and valid ID proof.
- Sign the **Locker Access Register** each time you access the locker.
- All locker operations are conducted in privacy in the vault area.

---

## Additional Guidelines

- **Nomination:** Highly recommended to nominate a beneficiary for your locker.
- **Joint Locker:** You can opt for a joint locker with another account holder.
- **Loss of Key:** Inform the bank immediately; charges apply for key duplication/drilling.
- **Annual Rent:** Pay annual rent on time to avoid late fees or locker freeze.
