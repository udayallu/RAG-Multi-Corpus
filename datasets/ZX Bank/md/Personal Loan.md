## Documentation Required

**For Salaried Individuals:**

- Completed Personal Loan Application Form
- Photo Identity Proof (Aadhaar Card, Passport, Voter ID, etc.)
- Address Proof (Utility bill, Passport, Aadhar, etc.)
- Latest 3 months’ Salary Slips
- Last 6 months’ Bank Statements
- 1 Passport-size Photograph
- PAN Card (for Indian applicants)

**For Self-Employed Individuals:**

- Completed Application Form
- Photo Identity & Address Proof
- Last 2 years’ Income Tax Returns
- Business Continuity Proof (Trade license, GST certificate, etc.)
- Last 6 months’ Bank Statements
- PAN Card (for Indian applicants)
