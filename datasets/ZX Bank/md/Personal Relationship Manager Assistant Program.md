# ZX Bank – Personal Relationship Manager Assistant Program

Welcome to the **Personal Relationship Manager (PRM) Assistant Program**, an exclusive service designed for all ZX Bank account holders. Our PRM Assistant transforms the way you bank—offering dedicated, professional, and tailored support for your daily financial needs.

---

## What is the PRM Assistant Program?

The Personal Relationship Manager Assistant Program connects you with a **dedicated relationship manager**—a friendly banking expert who understands your unique needs. Whether you manage your finances digitally or prefer in-person services, your PRM Assistant will guide and support you, making banking easier and more personalized than ever before.

---

##  Key Features and Benefits

- **Dedicated Support**
  - Get a named manager as your single point of contact for all banking queries.
  - Receive priority assistance for account-related concerns, service requests, and troubleshooting.

- **Efficient Account Management**
  - Assistance with day-to-day transactions: fund transfers, deposits, withdrawals, and more.
  - Personalized help with cheque books, debit/credit card management, and loan tracking.

- **Personalized Services**
  - Account usage insights, budgeting tips, and notification of important account activities.
  - Tailored recommendations on banking products and investment opportunities to suit your goals.

- **On-Demand Engagement**
  - Connect with your PRM via phone, email, scheduled video calls, or secure chat through ZX Bank Asia mobile app.
  - Direct appointment booking for in-branch meetings.

- **Proactive Updates**
  - Timely notifications about offers, maturity dates, and upcoming payments.
  - Regulatory and compliance alerts to keep you compliant and informed.

## Who Can Use the PRM Assistant?

**All ZX Bank account holders**, regardless of account type, automatically get access to a Personal Relationship Manager Assistant at no extra charge.

---

## How to Reach Your PRM Assistant

- **Via ZX Bank Asia App:** Tap ‘PRM Assistant’ for secure messaging or video calls.
- **By Phone:** Call your dedicated relationship manager directly (contact found in the app or welcome kit).
- **In Branch:** Schedule a face-to-face meeting at your nearest ZX Bank branch.
