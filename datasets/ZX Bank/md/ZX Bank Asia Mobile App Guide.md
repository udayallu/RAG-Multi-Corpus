# ZX Bank Asia Mobile App Guide

## Overview

The **ZX Bank Asia** mobile app is your gateway to seamless, secure, and smart banking. With support for Android and iOS, manage your finances on the go with intuitive banking features, advanced security, and real-time assistance.

---

## 1. How to Download the ZX Bank Asia App

**For Android:**
1. Open Google Play Store.
2. Search for: `ZX Bank Asia`.
3. Tap `Download` and install the app.

**For iOS:**
1. Open Apple App Store.
2. Search for: `ZX Bank Asia`.
3. Tap `Get` and install the app.

---

## 2. How to Activate Mobile Banking

1. **Open the ZX Bank Asia App**.
2. Tap on `Register / Activate`.
3. Enter your registered mobile number & customer ID/account number.
4. Authenticate via OTP sent to your mobile.
5. Set your preferred login PIN or configure biometric authentication (fingerprint/Face ID).
6. Complete e-KYC verification if prompted.

---

## 3. How to Login

- Open the app.
- Enter your PIN or use biometric login (if enabled).
- Access your dashboard securely.

---

## 4. App Features

### Account Management

- Check savings/current account balances.
- View transaction history and mini statements.
- Track FD, RD, and loan details in real-time.

### Fund Transfer

#### **Within ZX Bank / Other Banks**
1. Tap `Fund Transfer`.
2. Choose between ZX Bank or Other Bank (NEFT/IMPS/UPI).
3. Select/Add payee (see below).
4. Enter amount & remarks.
5. Confirm with PIN/OTP.

#### **Add Payee**
1. Go to `Fund Transfer` → `Manage Payees`.
2. Select `Add Payee`.
3. Enter payee's account info (name, bank details, IFSC) or UPI ID.
4. Verify via OTP.
5. Payee is added–start transferring.

### Bill Payments & QR

- Pay electricity, water, gas, mobile & credit card bills.
- Scan & pay using QR codes at merchants or for quick money transfers.

### Cards

#### **Check, Apply & Manage Credit/Debit Cards**
- View existing cards with usage summary, payment due dates, statements, and reward points.
- **Block Card**: Select card → More options → Block (immediate effect).
- **Reset Card PIN**: Select card → Change PIN → Enter old & new PIN.
- **Apply New Card**: Cards Menu → Apply for New Card → Choose credit or debit → Fill application & submit docs.

#### **Set Card Limits**
- Go to `Manage Cards`.
- Choose the card and tap `Set Transaction Limit`.
- Adjust for ATM, POS, online, contactless—save changes.

### Loans

- View all loans, EMIs, schedules & download statements.
- Apply for new loans: Home, personal, or vehicle → Fill form → Upload KYC → Track status.

### Rewards

- Check `Rewards` tab for current points.
- Redeem reward points instantly for vouchers, cashback, or gift cards.

### NRI & Wealth

- Access NRI services, mutual funds, wealth advisory, and initiate investments.

### Chatbot – **Zia**

- Ask Zia for help with account queries, product info, quick balance, and transaction support.
