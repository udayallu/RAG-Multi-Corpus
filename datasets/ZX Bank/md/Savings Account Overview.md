## How to Apply for a ZX Bank Savings Account

You can open a ZX Bank Savings Account easily through these channels:

### 1. **Via Mobile App or Website**
   - Download the **ZX Bank Asia** app (Android/iOS) or visit [www.zxbank.asia](https://www.zxbank.asia)
   - Click “Open Savings Account”
   - Fill in your details and upload necessary documents (PAN/ID, Address Proof)
   - Complete e-KYC via video or Aadhaar OTP
   - Account activation is done within minutes!

### 2. **At Branch**
   - Visit your nearest ZX Bank branch  
   - Submit KYC documents with a duly filled application form
   - Staff will assist you with on-the-spot e-KYC
   - Collect your Welcome Kit instantly
