# ZX Bank Car Loan

**Get your dream ride with ZX Bank’s easy and affordable Car Loan!**  
Whether you’re buying a new or used vehicle, ZX Bank’s flexible car loan solutions make it simple to drive away with competitive rates, fast approval, and hassle-free documentation.

---

## **Features & Benefits**

- **Loan Amount:** Up to ₹80 lakhs (India); equivalent amounts in other regions
- **Tenure:** 12 to 84 months
- **Attractive Interest Rates:** Starting from 8.25% p.a.*
- **Quick Disbursal:** Get funds within 48 hours of approval
- **Flexible Repayment:** EMI options tailored to your budget
- **All Vehicle Types:** New & pre-owned cars, electric vehicles eligible
- **No Hidden Charges:** Transparent processing fees and foreclosure terms
- **Pre-approved Offers:** For select ZX Bank customers

---

## **Eligibility**

- **Salaried individuals:** Age 21–60 | Minimum monthly income: ₹20,000 (varies by region)
- **Self-employed/professionals:** Age 21–65 | Minimum business vintage: 2 years

---

## **Documentation Required**

- **KYC Documents:** Aadhaar/Passport/Driving License, PAN, photograph
- **Income Proof:** Salary slips/ITR/Bank statements (Past 6 months)
- **Employment/Business Proof:** Offer letter/Business registration
- **Vehicle Quotation/Invoice**

_All documents can be uploaded digitally via the ZX Bank Asia app/NetBanking_

---

## **How to Apply**

### **Online (Recommended)**
1. **Download** the [ZX Bank Asia App](https://www.zxbank.asia/app) or log in to [ZX NetBanking](https://www.zxbank.asia/netbanking)
2. Go to **“Loans” → “Car Loan”**
3. **Fill the application** with your personal and vehicle details
4. **Upload documents** using e-KYC (Aadhaar/Passport), salary slips, etc.
5. **Check instant eligibility** and available offers
6. **Submit application** for quick processing and approval updates

### **Offline / Branch**
1. Visit your nearest [ZX Bank Branch](https://www.zxbank.asia/branches)
2. Meet a relationship manager for personalized assistance
3. Submit completed car loan application and documents
