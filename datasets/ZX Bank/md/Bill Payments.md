## Auto Debit Feature

**Never miss a payment!**  
Set up hassle-free auto debits from your ZX Bank account or credit card for recurring bills.

### How Auto Debit Works

1. **Register Online:**  
   Enable auto debit for your chosen billers via Mobile App or NetBanking.

2. **Custom Schedule:**  
   Select the payment date (due date or preferred day prior).

3. **Automated Payments:**  
   ZX Bank automatically processes payments on your behalf.

4. **Alerts & Notifications:**  
   Get SMS/email alerts before every scheduled auto debit.

5. **Manage & Cancel Anytime:**  
   Modify or stop auto debits anytime using the "Manage Mandates" section.

## How To Get Started

1. **Download the ZX Bank Asia Mobile App** (*Android/iOS*)
2. **Login** or complete e-KYC onboarding
3. **Navigate to “Bill Payments” or “Auto Debit”**  
   - Add your biller (credit card, mobile, DTH, etc.)
   - Choose “Enable Auto Debit” and set your preferences
4. **Confirm & Save** — you're done!
