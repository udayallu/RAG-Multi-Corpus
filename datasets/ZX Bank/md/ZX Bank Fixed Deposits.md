# ZX Bank Fixed Deposits

ZX Bank offers attractive and secure Fixed Deposit (FD) solutions tailored for individuals, businesses, and Non-Resident Indians (NRIs) across its operating countries. With ZX Bank's robust digital services and reliable branch presence, customers can enjoy seamless booking, management, and maturity payouts of fixed deposits.

---

## Why Choose ZX Bank Fixed Deposits?

- **Competitive Interest Rates:** Enjoy higher returns compared to regular savings accounts.
- **Flexible Tenures:** Tenure options ranging from 7 days to 10 years.
- **Safe & Secure:** Backed by ZX Bank’s sound financial position and regulatory compliance.
- **Loan Against FD:** Avail instant overdraft or loans up to 90% of your FD value.
- **Premature Withdrawal:** Facility available (premature closure charges may apply).
- **Digital Opening:** Open, renew, or close FDs instantly through ZX Bank Asia mobile app or NetBanking.

---

## Features & Benefits

- Minimum Deposit: ₹5,000 (India), LKR 10,000 (Sri Lanka), BDT 10,000 (Bangladesh), BTN 1,000 (Bhutan), NPR 1,500 (Nepal)
- Interest Payout: Choose from monthly, quarterly, or on maturity payout options
- Special Schemes: Senior citizen extra interest, tax-saver FDs, recurring deposit variants
- Joint Deposits: Facility for joint holding and nomination
- Auto-renewal: Hassle-free renewal option available

## How to Apply

1. **Via Mobile App:**  
   - Download the ZX Bank Asia app (Android/iOS)
   - Register/login and choose "Fixed Deposit"
   - Select amount, tenure, and nominee
   - Confirm with OTP/biometric authentication

2. **Via NetBanking:**  
   - Login to www.zxbank.asia
   - Navigate to "Deposits" → "Open Fixed Deposit"
   - Fill in required details and confirm

3. **At Branch:**  
   - Visit your nearest ZX Bank branch with KYC documents
   - Fill out the FD application form and deposit the amount
