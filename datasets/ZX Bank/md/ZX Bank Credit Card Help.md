# How to Close Your ZX Bank Credit Card

Closing your ZX Bank Credit Card is a straightforward process. Follow the steps below to ensure a smooth and secure closure:

---

## 1. **Clear Outstanding Dues**
- **Pay Off All Dues:** Settle any outstanding amounts (balances, fees, charges, or EMI on the card).
- **Download Statements:** Keep a record of your final statement for future reference.

---

## 2. **Redeem Unused Rewards**
- **Check for Points:** Redeem all reward points, cashback, or benefits, as they will be lost after closure.

---

## 3. **Cancel Auto-debits & Linked Payments**
- **Deactivate:** Cancel standing instructions, auto-debits, or subscriptions linked to your card.

---

## 4. **Request Card Closure**
You can place a closure request using any of the following channels:

### a. **Via ZX Bank Mobile App ("ZX Bank Asia")**
  1. Login to the app.
  2. Go to **Card Services** > **Credit Card Closure**.
  3. Select your card and follow the on-screen prompts.

### b. **Through ZX NetBanking**
  1. Login to ZX NetBanking.
  2. Navigate to **Cards** > **Credit Cards** > **Close Credit Card**.
  3. Select your credit card and confirm your request.

### c. **Customer Care**
  - Call 24x7 helpline:  
    - India: 1800-123-4567  
    - Sri Lanka: 011-2345678  
    - Bangladesh: 0961-234567  
  - Request card closure with your card details handy.

### d. **Branch Visit**
  - Visit your nearest ZX Bank branch.
  - Submit a written request or fill out a card closure form.

---

## 5. **Destroy the Card**
- **After confirmation,** cut your card (including chip and magnetic strip) and dispose of it securely.

---

## 6. **Obtain Closure Confirmation**
- **Closure Letter/Email:** ZX Bank will send a closure confirmation within 7 working days.
- **Check CIBIL/Credit Report:** Ensure the card appears as ‘closed’ (not ‘settled’).

---

## #### Important Notes
- Closing a credit card may affect your credit score.
- You cannot re-activate a closed card; a new application is required.
- If you see continuing charges post-closure, inform ZX Bank immediately.

For help, contact ZX Bank Customer Care via the **ZX Bank Asia** mobile app or visit [www.zxbank.asia](http://www.zxbank.asia).
