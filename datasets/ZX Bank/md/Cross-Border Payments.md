# Fast Cross-Border Payments with ZX Bank

### Seamless Transfers Across South Asia

ZX Bank provides **fast, secure cross-border payment services** between **India, Sri Lanka, Nepal, and Bhutan**. Whether you’re sending money home, paying for business imports, or supporting family across the region, ZX Bank guarantees a seamless remittance experience.

---

## Key Features

- **Instant Transfers:** Most transfers are processed within minutes using ZX Bank’s advanced payment rails and SWIFT capabilities.
- **Transparent Fees:** Competitive, upfront charges with no hidden costs.
- **Multi-Currency Support:** Send and receive funds in INR, LKR, NPR, BTN, and more.
- **Real-Time Tracking:** Track your payments via the ZX Bank Asia mobile app and get instant notifications.
- **Branch & Digital Access:** Initiate remittances through 600+ branches/ATMs or remotely using internet and mobile banking.
- **Regulatory Compliance:** Fully compliant with all local and international guidelines. Your transactions are protected!

---

## How It Works

1. **Initiate Transfer**
   - Visit a ZX Bank branch, use our mobile app, or log in to NetBanking.
2. **Choose Your Recipient**
   - Send to ZX Bank or partner bank accounts in India, Sri Lanka, Nepal, or Bhutan.
3. **Select Amount & Currency**
   - Multi-currency options help recipients get the best value.
4. **Pay & Confirm**
   - Transparent, upfront fees shown before you pay.
5. **Track in Real-Time**
   - Monitor your transfer’s status from start to finish.
