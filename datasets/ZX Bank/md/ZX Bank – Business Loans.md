## Types of Business Loans Offered

### 1. **Working Capital Loans**
> Manage day-to-day expenses, purchase inventory, cover receivables, or fund operations.

### 2. **Term Loans**
> For business expansion, asset purchase, or infrastructure development. Available both as short-term and long-term tenures.

### 3. **SME Loans**
> Special category for micro, small, and medium enterprises with minimal paperwork and attractive rates.

### 4. **Trade Finance Solutions**
> Letters of Credit, Bank Guarantees, Bill Discounting, and other export/import funding.

### 5. **Women Empowerment Loans**
> Tailored loans for women entrepreneurs and women-led businesses across South Asia.

### 6. **Green & Sustainability Loans**
> Financing for renewable energy, eco-friendly infrastructure, and climate-smart business initiatives.

## How to Apply?

1. **Visit:** Any ZX Bank branch in your country, or apply online via [ZX Bank Asia App](https://www.zxbank.asia).
2. **Eligibility:** The following may apply—
   - Proprietorships, Partnerships, Private/Public Limited Companies, Start-ups, Women Entrepreneurs
   - Meets bank’s KYC and credit criteria
3. **Documents:** Easy digital submission:
   - Business proof, Financial statements, KYC documents, Project report (if applicable)
4. **Approval & Disbursal:** Fast-track approval with door-step service for eligible applicants.
5. **Manage:** Track disbursal, EMI, and statements with the ZX Bank Asia app.
